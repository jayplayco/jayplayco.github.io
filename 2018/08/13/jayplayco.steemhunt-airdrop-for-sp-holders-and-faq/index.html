[Steemhunt] Airdrop for SP holders and FAQ

Hi, @jayplayco here with a fast update about the new Airdrop for SP holders from Steemhunt and with the most asked questions.

  • Q. How can I claim my Hunt tokens for SP
    A. Please read the announcements from @steemhunt as it is explained there.

  • Q. What is the value of the Hunt Token?
    A. The Hunt tokens are not yet transferable, so there can’t exactly a price be measured at the moment, and the end prices are made from the market.

  • Q. When will the token be transferable?
    A. It is planned for the week of the 20th of September (so it could take up to the 26th of September.) to make the Hunt token ready for ERC-20. You would then be able to move your Hunt Tokens to a personal ERC-20 compatible wallet. After moving it out to an ERC-20 Wallet, you will need a small Amount of Ethereum for Gas to move it back to somewhere.

  • Q. Do I have to move the Hunt tokens when the ERC-20 is available?
    A. Not really, as the primary plan is at the end to swap the Hunt Tokens later to SMT tokens, which will be in the moment most comfortable when they are still in the Steemhunt wallet.

  • Q. When are Hunt Tokens listed on an exchange?
    A. It will be done automatically when we have enough community power. Steemhunt is not planning to pay exchanges for listings like other partly scammy ICO’s without a product behind and will further continue to ship code and develop the product and the community. So the answer is, bring in more great hunters into our community and you will see your hunt tokens grow faster and be listed more quickly on an exchange.

  • Q. Are the airdrop tokens for my SP added to the Hunt Tokens I already have?
    A. Yes, they are added.

  • Q. When I claim my Tokens now and have more SP next week, can I claim again?
    A. No, it is a one Time claim only.

  • Q. I heard the Airdrop is first come first serve, will I be too late?
    A. Don’t worry too much about that, as the airdrop is covering 100M Hunt tokens, which is almost equivalent to the SP’s hold by users (excluding Steemit Inc holdings). But the Airdrop runs for only one month and will be finished on September 13th 2018, so you should not wait too long for claiming your Hunt Tokens.

So what you are waiting for? Move to www.steemhunt.com and claim your Hunt Tokens now!
