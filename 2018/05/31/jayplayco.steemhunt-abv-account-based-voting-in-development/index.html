2. Yeah, let`s use ABV, but what is that?

The account-based voting revolution - @liberosist
Account Based Voting is a Terrible Idea for STEEM - @nealmcspadden

@ned has mentioned ABV (Account based voting) on his Korea tour and there are pros and cons on it, but to explain it easily see below,

A. Current voting system.

  • The more steem power you have, more voting power you get, which means you can give more money.
  • problem : bots, curation groups, curation trails, voting pools.

B. Account based voting

  • One account counts has only one voting power
  • problem : without any weighting of an account a spam account has the same power as a whale account. This is what @nealmcspadden is talking about.

3. Approach from Steemhunt team / New Features

github entry : https://github.com/Steemhunt/web/issues/135

Logic

  • One upvote has 10 points with 100% vp.
  • The point proportionally decrease based on the hunter’s set vp. For example, the hunter upvotes with 50% vp, the score will be 5 point.
  • Whitelist: Scores are only be counted by the user who have hunted AT LEAST ONE TIME.

Possible Progress (Just examples and not fixed)

  • More intensive whitelist: counted by the hunters with at least three times, exclude blacklist hunters, etc.
  • Hunter’s badge system: We can level the hunters based on their hunting history, from Lv.1 to Lv.5.

Lv.1: 1 hunts (1 full voting score: 10 poinst)
Lv.2: 10 days hunting + 10 times upvoting the hunting posts (1 full voting score: 20 poinst)
Lv.3: Lv.2 + at least 3 times on top 10 hunts (1 full voting score: 30 poinst)
Lv.4: Lv.2 + at least 5 times on top 10 hunts (1 full voting score: 50 poinst)
Lv.5: 30 days hunting + 30 times upvoting the hunting posts + at least 10 times on top 10 hunts. (1 full voting score: 100 poinst)
The Lv can be downgraded if the hunter does not have any hunting or voting activities more than 10 days. (Lv 5 -> Lv 4 -> …)

4. So what does that mean?

@steemhunt will give only active Steemhunters the possibility to vote (whitelist) and level them with badges to differentiate the reputation within the community. Based on the given voting power% a vote can count from 0-100. (Based on your level) The badge system gives different levels to activate the hunters for better content. All this steps are planned to give active hunters with high quality content the best chance for the highest rewards.

5. Roadmap

It is planned to test the first settings next week, but there is in this moment no official deadline from team @steemhunt , but the main dev. from Steemhunt mentioned that it is very a high priority before scaling to the next stage. So let`s see how this will change the quality of the content at steemhunt.
