Hunter’s comment

Spigen is a well know company for high quality smartphone cases.
This time they have started an indiegogo campaign for a real retro nostalgic cover case.

It is an iPhone case for the iPhone with dual Layer and in the retro look of an iMac G3.

https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmZqvCxhiHKz8oTzcEoGRz6Atevzi7rZ81soXQpzitFuWz/image.png

The case is still slim enough to keep the wireless charging function active.

The price for 25USD is not really cheap, but as Spigen comes in normal cases with a high quality case, your money should be invested wisely here.

If you had an iMac G3 or liked the design from that time, don`t wait and grap your piece, as the deal goes for 14 days.
