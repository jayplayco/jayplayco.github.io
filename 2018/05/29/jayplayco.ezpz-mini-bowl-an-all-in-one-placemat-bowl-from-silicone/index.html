Hunter’s comment

Ezpz has been introduced 2014 and started at that time with a successful Kickstarter.

The main idea behind it is to combine the placemat with the bowl. Due to the suction the bowl is not anymore removeable for children and so mom and dad can give the food without having any concerns.

Due to the fact that this product is out of pure high quality food grade silicone, you can dishwash it, get in into the oven or even boil it in water for disinfection if needed.
