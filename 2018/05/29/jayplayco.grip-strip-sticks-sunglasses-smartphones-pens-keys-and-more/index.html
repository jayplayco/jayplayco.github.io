Hunter’s comment

Using polymer compound silicone this material grips everything flat on the surface. Ideal for using it in the car and stick your phone, cards or keys on it.

The stickiness can be recovered when flushing it in clean water.

For a price of 6.99 UK Pounds it looks like a bit pricy gadget, but if you are not using high quality silicone on this kind of product, I have seen melting grip strips in the car.

Guess for this price this grip strip will be alright.
