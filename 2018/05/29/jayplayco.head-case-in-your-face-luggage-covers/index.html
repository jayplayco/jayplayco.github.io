Being on travel is (almost) always fun but I guess everybody remembers the time when standing at the luggage carousel and realizing that almost every luggage is either black or silver.

This product is here to get you out of this kind of situation, as it is a luggage cover with your own face (or from someone else) printed on it. So nobody will be able to snug your luggage and claim it is theirs~.

The head cases are made from polyester spandex so they stretch over the bag.

All you need is a high quality photo from your (or from someone else) to be uploaded on the site.
