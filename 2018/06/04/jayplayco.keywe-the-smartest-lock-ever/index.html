Product Description

A Door smart lock having safety as No1. priority. It is build with Ansi Grade 2 deadbolt and aluminium. Also a 128 bit AES encryption for the connection between the lock and the app.

Unique Selling Proposition

-Auto Lock & Unlock - Touchpad with 20 passcodes - Onetime Passcode available - Guest Key available - NFC included - Also with Mechanical Key
