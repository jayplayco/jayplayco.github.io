Product Story

A really analog product was born. Don't you often try to remember a special moment? This product was really born for such a moment. Pull the handle to start an analog clock and remember the moment until 2738. Of course, the batteries last only 20 years, but since the batteries can be replaced at any time without losing time, it would be great if the astronauts could remember this moment until they come.

The product has a handle that ends with a pull and two LCDs. The preceding LCD indicates the year, and the latter shows the time.

Unique Selling Proposition

*Building a 2,738 year timepiece. *Materials selected for durability and legibility. *Electronics specified for longevity and low power consumption *A 20+ year battery life *A real time chip with ±2ppm accuracy. *Safely change batteries without losing time.
