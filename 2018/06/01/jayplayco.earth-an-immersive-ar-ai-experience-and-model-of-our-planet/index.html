Hunter’s comment

Earth globes are something really fascinating.

EARTH has taken this to the next level adding AR and AI per App looking into the globe.

So you can explorer the globe not only in 3D but also 4D within time. That is really cool.

  1. Earth globe scaled at 1:106,300,000
  2. A lot of data points for AI and AR
  3. Carbon Dioxide Distribution across time
  4. Migration of animals in time
  5. Biomes and crops and key plants in the world
  6. changing geology
  7. Climate change
  8. Anthropology and human experience
  9. Weather system.
