EARTH - An immersive AR/AI experience & model of our planet

EARTH

An immersive AR/AI experience & model of our planet

Screenshots

![a7dedf5ae4b110397d327e03e56f0bd6_original.gif](https://i.imgur.com/wsLmynM.gif)

Hunter’s comment

Earth globes are something really fascinating.

EARTH has taken this to the next level adding AR and AI per App looking into the globe.

So you can explorer the globe not only in 3D but also 4D within time. That is really cool.

  1. Earth globe scaled at 1:106,300,000
  2. A lot of data points for AI and AR
  3. Carbon Dioxide Distribution across time
  4. Migration of animals in time
  5. Biomes and crops and key plants in the world
  6. changing geology
  7. Climate change
  8. Anthropology and human experience
  9. Weather system.

https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmP6AAnNeSDb6rZnRc8dfieneTy5cE29WSpuZZ7oDXYBak/image.png

The app has also an intergrated voice system (like alexa or siri) called Gaya. It helps to ask different questions about the EARTH.

https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmV6Bk9QZT92kfvN9LNpFaK5FTcU3u8wZq5APXRUWoKFXx/image.png

Specification of EARTH are like followed.
https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmWZwTE66vL3RZFZnwN5wNaPC3aTbd7bjPuKZ2nZ2s8Ued/image.png

At a price level of 199USD for an earth globe, the price looks like heavy, but with all the AR and AI information in the backhand per APP it is a great education tool for your kids and a nice nice nice toy for yourself.

This project is saying “Shut up and take my money~!”

*Note : I REALLY love earth globes :)

April - Paint by Numbers iOS App

April

Paint by Numbers iOS App

Screenshots

![april.jpg](https://i.imgur.com/GCQh9Lk.jpg)
![april01.JPG](https://i.imgur.com/HEdnS5U.jpg)
![april02.JPG](https://i.imgur.com/LxIsh33.jpg)

Hunter’s comment

Get stressed? No time at all?
Stop what you are doing and just relax for a second.

There are a lot of coloring books and apps outside, but this one is giving a bit of guidance with numbering on each part. This means that you get better results as when you just start to paint whatever you want.

The basic app is for free and it offers In-App purchases.

You have in the free version hundreds of illustrations which are separated in galleries and also levels. A sharing mode is also available to show what you have done.

https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/april/id1360270159?mt=8&ref=steemhunt

Spigen Classics - iPhone Case inspired by iMac G3

Spigen Classics

iPhone Case inspired by iMac G3

Screenshots

![spigen.gif](https://i.imgur.com/nkI86er.gif)

Hunter’s comment

Spigen is a well know company for high quality smartphone cases.
This time they have started an indiegogo campaign for a real retro nostalgic cover case.

It is an iPhone case for the iPhone with dual Layer and in the retro look of an iMac G3.

https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmZqvCxhiHKz8oTzcEoGRz6Atevzi7rZ81soXQpzitFuWz/image.png

The case is still slim enough to keep the wireless charging function active.

The price for 25USD is not really cheap, but as Spigen comes in normal cases with a high quality case, your money should be invested wisely here.

If you had an iMac G3 or liked the design from that time, don`t wait and grap your piece, as the deal goes for 14 days.

LG Rolly Keyboard 2 - Second version of rollable portable keyboard from LG

LG Rolly Keyboard 2

Second version of rollable portable keyboard from LG

Screenshots

![lg_rolly.jpg](https://i.imgur.com/41XOx2y.jpg)

Hunter’s comment

If you are using a laptop, tablet or even a bigger smartphone, sometimes you need to write longer text on this products. A laptop should be not a problem, but as soon as we start to type longer text on touch screens my productivity is going down quite fast.

Bluetooth keyboards are normaly very bulky, so I don`t carry them around but this Rolly Keyboard from LG can be rolled and it means that the product is really small and easy to carry around.

Based on Bluetooth HD profie and a rolled out dimension of 10.78”x1.24”x1.20” it runs up to 3.5 month with one AAA battery.

The first version of this product was quite expensive for a bluetooth keyboard and the official retail price is 100USD, but you can get it now also on amazon for about 45USD, which is OK.

Link

http://www.lg.com/us/mobile-accessories/lg-KBB-710-rolly-keyboard-2?ref=steemhunt

Ezpz Mini Bowl - An all-in-one placemat + bowl from Silicone

Ezpz Mini Bowl

An all-in-one placemat + bowl from Silicone

Screenshots

![ezpz-web-product-shots-Mini-Bowl-Blush-3__02106.1513114649.1280.800.jpg](https://i.imgur.com/fGV0hm8.jpg)

Hunter’s comment

Ezpz has been introduced 2014 and started at that time with a successful Kickstarter.

The main idea behind it is to combine the placemat with the bowl. Due to the suction the bowl is not anymore removeable for children and so mom and dad can give the food without having any concerns.

Due to the fact that this product is out of pure high quality food grade silicone, you can dishwash it, get in into the oven or even boil it in water for disinfection if needed.

https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmXuJSFRYdei1jwDLtzKsw19VKAeejn7FFZ3VrpV5qN391/image.png
