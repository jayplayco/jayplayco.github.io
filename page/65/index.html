[스팀픽] 증인 노드 운영

안녕하세요, @jayplayco 입니다.

오랜만에 스팀픽 관련 소식을 들고 왔습니다.

https://steempeak.com/

다름이 아니라 스팀픽측이 어제부로 공식적으로 증인노드를 운영하고 있다고 선포를 했습니다. (물론 서버는 이미 이전 부터 돌고 있긴 했습니다만, 스팀페스트와 같이 공표하는 것을 목표로 해서 이제 공식 발표를 했네요.)

  • 스팀피크 관련 소개 내용을 보시려면
    https://steempeak.com/kr/@jayplayco/steempeak

  • 공식적인 공지는 하기와 같습니다.
    https://steempeak.com/witness/@steempeak/announcement-the-new-steempeak-witness-new-witness-features

1. 왜 스팀픽은 증인을 잘 할 수 있을까?

제가 개인적으로 생각하는 점들을 정리하겠습니다.

  • 스팀픽은 현재 스팀기반의 스팀잇을 가장 잘 표현한 3rd 파티입니다.
  • 예쁘고, 빠르고, 필요한 거의 모든 기능을 지원합니다.
  • 개발진과 팀이 다양한 아이디어로 빠르게 개선점들을 적용해나가고 있습니다.
  • 스팀픽 운영진은 피크몬도 같이 운영합니다. (피크몬com과 피크몬kr)
  • 피크몬에서 나오는 수익 일부가 스팀픽 운영에 쓰여지는 관계로, 우선 서버 운영에 필요한 비용 걱정이 없다
  • 증인 서버에서 나오는 모든 수익은 증인 서버 운영비와 스팀피크 개발 비용에 쓸 예정임.

2. 스팀픽이 뛰어난 것을 보여주는 예시

이번 증인 출시와 같이 스팀픽에서 보여지는 증인 페이지를 전격 개선(증설)했습니다. 그간 증인 보팅및 찾기 등 정말 불편했던 사항들을 말끔하게 정리해서 스팀픽 내부의 한페이지에서 깔끔하게 정리해두었습니다.

https://steempeak.com/witnesses

직접가서 확인하는것이 가장 빠르긴 하지만, 중요한 기능은 하기와 같이 정리합니다.

  • UI 개선 (모든 중요 data표기)
  • 전체 랭킹 표시 가능 (100위까지가 아니라 전체 순위 다 보임)
  • 필터 기능 (증인 활성/비활성, 투표한 증인만 보여주기)
  • 남은 투표권 확인 (로그인시)
  • 투표시 Steemconnect 열어줌 (active 키 입력)
  • 이름으로 찾기 기능
  • 버젼으로 찾기 기능
  • Params 보기 기능 (블록 사이즈, AC budget등)
  • 증인을 돌리고 있는 어카운트의 경우 Profile에 증인 버튼 추가 (증인 하고 계시는 분들은 그냥 스팀픽 자기 프로파일로 링크를 걸어주시면 쉽게 증인 투표를 할 수 있습니다. 오홋!)

3. 서버사양

일부 궁금해하시는 분들이 계셔서 같이 공지해드립니다. ^^

전용 서버 (Dedicated Server)

* CPU: 4 Core @ 3.60 GHz * RAM: 64GB DDR3 RAM * HDD: 2x 250GB SSD * OS: Ubuntu 16.04

소프트웨어

* steemd의 개별 build를 이용해 가능한 가장 빠른 response time 구현. * @therealwolf의 pricefeed script 적용 * 자체 증인 모니터링 소프트웨어 개발로 이메일 경고 시스템 구축

백업 서버

랭킹이 예비에서 증인으로 올라가게 되면 백업 서버 증설 예정임.

5. 투표하기

스팀픽에 대한 믿음이 생기셨다면 증인투표를 하기 위해서 여러가지 방식이 있습니다.

이상과 같이 스팀픽 증인 출마에 대한 정리를 드렸습니다. 스팀 기반 좋은 댑과 3rd 파티들이 자리 잡아서 더욱더 활기차고 좋은 스팀 환경을 만들어줄 팀들을 상위 랭킹에 올리는 것이 좋다고 생각합니다. ^^

Steemhunt Presentation

Hello, everyone, my name is Young Hwi, but my Steemit name is @project7, so probably it’s much easier for you guys to call me project7. I’m a designer and co-founder of Steemhunt.

I hope that many of you here already know what Steemhunt is but for those who don’t know much about it I like to give a very brief introduction about what Steemhunt is. So basically, Steemhunt is a community with a bunch of geeky techy people, who share some cool new innovative tech products and then compete with others like, “hey who did share the cooler and newer stuff than others” and later on a daily basis there’s a ranking chart created and curated by all the techy people. This has been a very successful community so far because of all the support from Steemians. Thank you very very much for all that support!

I want to show that the most important part of Steemhunt’s value is that we don’t want to create just a good community. There are already a lot of great communities in Steemit. But we want to utilize those community forces to be an asset. You guys are great users in that community, but probably someone else wants to use that as an asset.

Lots of platforms just utilized it for advertisement or display ads. This is also good, but it can be much better. So that’s why we invented the Hunt token economy, that promotes both sites, hunters, and makers. Who wants to utilize hunters force as an asset, hunts token will be rewarded to those hunters activities, so hunters keep sharing cool new innovative stuff constantly. That will promote more traffic outside of the Steemit community and will also attract all the makers and companies. If a maker wants to utilize the forces from hunters to boost up marketing, then they’re going to invite more and more users into the Hunt platform. That’s going to reward hunters what will increase more hunters actions. That results in more traffic and more makers. So this is our growth vehicle, and that will make us different from all the others.

That’s why we’re creating the Hunt platform. We’re not going just to staying as a Steemhunt community, but we’re creating Ideahunt and Reviewhunt on top of the Steemhunt community. We are already working on Reviewhunt, and the target launch will be April next year. Hunters always dig out new stuff, and they have a lot of knowledge about tech products. Some makers want to utilize their force to boost up their new product launching. They can suggest a large discount deal for hunters or they can give Hunt (token) rewards to them. Hunters will then check the products, and they’re going to create very high-quality reviews for them.

The biggest benefit for the makers to utilize our platform is that they can involve all the hunters as users. Anyone who was involved in an early startup knows how painful it is to get high-quality users in the very initial stage. This is going to be a very strong platform for those innovative makers who want access to that kind of high-quality content.

Idea hunt is the last milestone in our roadmap. But we strongly believe that this is going to be a game changer as a crowdfunding platform. Ideahunt is a crowdfunding platform on top of the Steemhunt community. We’ve noticed that current crowdfunding platforms have a major issue, especially Kickstarter. An example, if you backed a project and you need to pay right away. Then you wait until the product is shipped or not. You forget about that. We don’t think that this is efficient. If you back a project then you have to increase the value of the product even before they launched. At this moment we can’t disclose all the details but we are going to introduce it very soon. One point is that we are going to utilize Non-fungible token (NFT) transactions for Ideahunt. With NFT an increase of the backer’s actions can increase the value of the new product launch. This is going to be a real game changer in this industry.

Our ambitious plan requires to have lots of strong users. That’s why we initiated a quite unique way of distributing our Hunt token. By distributing 50% of airdrops. The core idea of this plan is that we want to connect token holders to be the users. Lots of ICO’s are based on the fact that token holders are just investors. They’re not likely to be connected to be a user. That’s not what we wanted to do. So that’s why we have a very unique token distribution. Still, around 24% is distributed, so there is plenty of chance to join this airdrop.

We also started a witness node a month ago with an ambitious mission as a witness. We want to make the Steem Blockchain a place that is favorable for Dapps and SMT’s. Then we can invite a lot of companies to be there. So please support us here. When we approach the market for HUNT tokens, we also want to approach the SMT wide protocol. Final goal would be that SMT tokens can be listed together. This is our aim.

We have some interesting news today as we are discussing with DAYBIT, which is a recently launched exchange market, but already almost in the top 15 level, globally. We are discussing to list Hunt token probably at the end of this year or beginning of the next year. Still ongoing communication so we can’t confirm yet, but we’re going to give you very exciting news very soon. Thank you so much

[스몬] 스팀몬스터 뉴비 지원 프로젝트

안녕하세요, @jayplayco 입니다.

이미 많은 분들이 스팀내에서 스팀몬스터 관련 소식을 많이 접하셨으리라 믿고 있습니다. 스팀기반 댑들이 많이 나오면 그만큼 스팀 생태계에는 긍정적으로 적용될것이라고 생각됩니다.

지금까지 스팀 댑들중 현금흐름을 가장 빠르고 많이 확보한 프로젝트가 바로 스팀몬스터인데요, 스팀몬스터 역시 모든 스팀 댑들이 갖고 있는 숙제중 하나인 신규 사용자 유입이 가장 큰 관건입니다.

KR내에서는 이미 많은 분들이 시작을 하셔서 랭킹에서도 좋은 성적을 내고 계시고 실제 지난 시즌 리그에서는 KR에서 1등 상금인 100SBD를 타가는 영예가 있었죠. (@smonster 화이팅!)

결국 가장 중요한 것은 더욱더 많은 분들이 스팀몬스터를 적극적으로 하시는데 있기 때문에 이번에는 뉴비 지원 프로젝트를 시작합니다.

1. 스몬 뉴비지원프로젝트

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmdu5Bi5A32n7PtV7GD8Q8y5hNf82kxWT8rADj58PJdSrg/image.png) [Source: Filip Mroz](https://unsplash.com/photos/oko_4WnoM98)

아직 한번도 스팀몬스터를 시작하지 않으신 분들, 관심은 있었는데 아직 엄두를 못 내신 분들을 KR내에서 모십니다.

2. 지원 방법및 혜택 -[이벤트 마감^^]

  • 총 비용 10SBD정도가 들어갑니다. 물론 신용카드로 사셔도 무관하고요 ^^. 스팀 아이디가 없을 경우 필요한 경우 새로운 아이디도 생성해줍니다.

  • 새롭게 가입하신 분들께는 뉴비 지원 차원에서 게임을 하실 수 있도록 부스터팩 5개를 보내드립니다. (시가 10USD)입니다.
  • 이걸로 사실상 스몬을 무료로 시작할 수 있는 방법을 열어드립니다. ^^
  • 총 10분을 모십니다. (수정해야했습니다. 팩이 모자르네요 ㅠㅠ)
  • 기간은 이 포스팅의 페이아웃이나 10분 다 모이실때까지입니다.

b. 제한 및 유의사항

  • 기본 플레이어분들의 부계정이나 새로 생성한 계정은 제외하겠습니다. (신규 유입자를 위함이니 ^^)

[Steemhunt] What is Steemhunt?

Hi, @jayplayco here.
This time I would like to introduce Steemhunt for people coming outside of Steem. It would be great to resteem it not only internally of Steem but also outside on platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and co. :)

0. Repository

https://github.com/Steemhunt/web

1. What is Steemhunt?

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmdmrqQpZKvSTnjwYxz9GcE9MCUpcQckW9hE1ooKEYwKVQ/image.png)

URL: https://steemhunt.com/

Steemhunt is a community-based platform for product influencers. Users here are called hunters. As a hunter, you can hunt cool and new products which compete on a ranking board on a daily basis. Other hunters can upvote the hunts when they like them. Each hunted product has the chance to climb to the top of the daily ranking. The best part is that the upvotes from other hunters are worth something, as you can gain a currency called STEEM(or SBD) with each upvote. If you want, you can change STEEM(or SBD) to Bitcoin (or similar) and then to USD on different exchanges.

If you love gadgets or looking for inspirations, this is the right place to be.

2. I am in, how should I start?

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmQtBgZVBygHjVRxezEvarPzc3rES8MG9AY7o1MgkGXaW2/image.png)

First of all, you will need to signup on Steemhunt.

  • You are creating a so-called STEEM account. Think of it as a Google OAuth like login for multiple services. Compared to Google, you can use on some web pages your Google account to log in. With this STEEM account, you can log in to other STEEM services.
  • Look for the Signup button and click on it.
  • Choose a username
  • Enter your mobile phone number and an SMS will be sent to you
  • Enter the confirmation code received on your mobile phone
  • Confirm it and you will get to a confirmation screen with your password.
  • STOP
    Copy your password, as you will never see it again!!!
  • It is still essential to copy your password and keep it safe.
  • Steemhunt is not able to retrieve your password. If you have copied your password, you can click on continue.
  • You are now able to log in.

3. I have logged in and now?

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmeNmqq5SquciLWVpjJWYcU3Q7YMmPbbSHdF1kQhPrZKnU/image.png)

There are several activities you can do. Go for hunting a new product or look for inspirations and upvote products you like. Or move over to Discord where other hunters are chatting with each other.

a. Hunting a product

Hunting a product is not difficult. Look for cool products and post them according to the posting guideline. A Steemhunt moderator will review your hunt manually, which can take up to 48h and leave a comment if the hunt is verified or not. If not reviewed on the same day it will move to the next day ranking board.

Hunters can upvote your hunt on Steemhunt when not delisted from a moderator. Each upvote does not count with the same weight. There is a hunter level in place which decides how strong a vote is. If you can get votes from seasoned hunters with higher levels, your hunt will have a better chance to rank higher.

Some points you should care about before you start.

  • Don`t look for products that have been hunted. (Use the search function to avoid duplicates)
  • Steemhunt only allows specific categories of products. You can check them here.
  • Don`t copy and paste text from other places.
  • It is recommended to read the Posting Guideline to understand what is allowed and what is not.

b. Upvoting other hunts

If you are not directly looking into hunting products, you can also help other hunters and upvote cool products. Just look for the "Today" section and press the arrow up button for a hunt.
![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmebQZoMupPWt4279yBuSoPJhEKVss5ogLSjMSFNxLSKDb/image.png)

You should not upvote more than ten hunts per day in the first moment. (There is a technical background, but on we will talk about it on a later article)

You can also comment on a hunt you like, but also don`t comment on more than one hunt per day in the beginning. (Yes, also a technical background here)

c. Chatting with other hunters on

Intermediate communication between hunters is happening only partly on the Steemhunt platform. A lot of hunters using a group chat service called Discord. You can join the group chat for Steemhunt [here for free.](https://discord.gg/mWXpgks)

Use the same username you have used for Steemhunt and look for the channels “#questions” if you need to ask something or “#discussions” if you have ideas about Steemhunt or want to read more about what other hunters are thinking about Steemhunt. “#faq” is an excellent channel to give you answers for most frequent questions.

4. I like it, but is there more?

Yes, and congratulation. You are now using a D'app on the Steem Blockchain. And you are mining your first cryptocurrencies with upvoting and hunting products. Understanding the technical background and meaning of the Steem Blockchain is at least a few articles for itself. But for now, have fun using Steemhunt and looking for great products. If you get used to Steemhunt and have more questions, you will find a good list of articles below to start to read.

Steemhunt is a place for Geeks and gadget lovers. And the system is designed to reward good hunters.
