[Steemhunt] Own Sign Up system

Hi, @jayplayco here.
Steemhunt is running smoothly, and RC mana is full again. Most of the hunters are now able to hunt and curate on Steemhunt again. As HF-20 is now “successfully” implemented, it is time to plan the next steps.

0. Repository

https://github.com/Steemhunt/web

1. DAU and MAU

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmaMoQXfkcirm3uo2so3WpukTRAeVRMufx9KQ2XnsCNV2J/image.png)
Steemhunt is snowballing, and after introducing the Social share [Airdrop](https://steemit.com/steemhunt/@jayplayco/steemhunt-category-of-airdrops) the DAU (Daily Active User) and MAU(Monthly Active User) numbers are increasing.

At the beginning of September MAU was on a level of about 10K but is now close to 60K.

2. Next needed steps?

One of the main issues Steem based Dapps has, is to get the attention from users outside Steem. If you have managed it well, the Steem community is very open for new Dapps or SMT projects. But your user number stops at a specific moment because of the limitation of the Steem user base. The difficulty starts when you want to outgrow your service from the Steem user base.

3. Limited User base

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmQsL46nxfZoctnYGhy4Go4A6fDvg5YusVuKaXoNQNBitN/image.png) [Source: @arcange](https://steemit.com/statistics/@arcange/steemit-statistics-20181003-en)

If we have a look into the numbers of active authors, it is stagnating, and the active unique users are also decreasing on a level above 100K per month.

With an MAU of about 60K at Steemhunt, it is needed to get users outside of the Steem economy.

4. Current Status

Steemhunt started already with different activities like Social Share airdrop or promotion contribution airdrops and these activities helped to boost the MAU now up to 60K.

But to convert visitors to users, you will need to get them signed to Steem. You can imagine several points that make this problematic.

  • Waiting time to create an account
  • Difficult and long password system for Non-Blockchain users
  • Login into a different site (Steemit)

Landing pages have a bounce rate of 60-90%. A login site on a separate site with a waiting time for two weeks or even paying for it would result probably into a bouncing rate of 99%.

5. HF20 and RC Mana

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmP4WUJhnoMS35q5yHEvTK1aEfWGWsauFJgtr1nq5w96qz/image.png) [Source: Bailey Heedick](https://unsplash.com/photos/yua5-HkEZus)

With the introduction of HF20 and RC Mana, it is now possible to use this Mana to create accounts. This change is very important for Dapps like Steemhunt. It would have cost at least three Steem for each account to produce it on your own. Based on a user base of 200K accounts, you would need at least 600K Steem. This is about 600K USD in the “current” moment. Five months ago you needed more than one Million USD only for account creations.

Now with RC Mana and enough Steem power (delegated or owned), you can use your RC Mana not only for interactions within the Steem Blockchain. The cost of creating an account is high when compared with other activities as writing an article or upvoting. You can create only a limited number of accounts per day because of your limited RC Mana. But even this limited number of accounts will increase the accessibility of users.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmajfx2TZtpBCfRzGRJmh6oyZpQjtM4yXfQ8Fu93B8r4zk/image.png)

Steemhunt will need a separate Sign Up page that is not forwarding to Steemit to keep the users on Steemhunt. The Sign Up process should be as less irritating as possible to avoid that the user cancel the sign up.

After peaking into the Discord channel, it seems that the new signup process has already been planned and the launch is already next week.

Having limited numbers of accounts per day should also not limit the actions of Steemhunt. I am sure that the team will find an exciting way of gamification to use this limit as an accelerator. It could be based on the thinking of, “High demand creates desire.”

As soon as this “Steemhunt Login System” is ready, Steemhunt will have to start a separate marketing campaign or Airdrop targeting new user acquisition. Some ideas could be the already announced Referral Airdrop or real targeted Facebook marketing. The important part is that it would be now possible to organize and start this kind of activities.

3. Conclusion

With a current MAU of about 60K, a bald goal for this year should be at least to reach an MAU of 300K. A milestone of 300K MAU is going to be an essential step also for the upcoming SMT. We could see a different kind of blockchain economies like EOS and Ethereum being very successful because of their abilities to launch significant and relevant projects. @ned, I am very confident that Steemhunt is and will be one of these projects carrying SMT to a success. We will get the investors interested in Steemhunt and at the end in the Steem Blockchain.

“Keep the hunt on”

[DCLICK] 스팀 SMT 기반 구글 애드센스 (광고시스템) 도입

안녕하세요, @jayplayco 입니다.

이미 많은 분들이 하고 계셔서 스팀 KR내에서 사용하신 분들도 임대를 시작하신 분들도 많으신걸로 알고 있습니다. 가장 빨리 정보를 전달 드리지는 않아도 심도깊게 알려드리고자 약간 시간을 두었습니다.

1. DCLICK이란 @dclick

2018년 10월 2일 첫 포스팅으로 세상에 알려진 서비스입니다. [공지 보기.](https://steemit.com/dclick/@dclick/dclick-an-incentivized-ad-platform-by-proof-of-click-) 가장 쉬운 설명은 스팀의 SMT 기반의 구글 애드센스입니다. 가장 큰 장점은 광고주가 초저비용으로 광고를 할 수 있다는 것입니다.

2. DCLICK의 백그라운드

디클릭은 @interesteem을 개발하신 @kws4679님의 작품입니다. #KR의 실질적인 Angel Investor이신 @clayop님께 pitching을 해서 약 50K의 Steem을 임대받고 런칭을 했습니다. 소개후 KR내에서 추가로 약 70분의 임대를 받아 현재는 2틀만에 111,003SP의 범고래가 되어있습니다.

100% 보팅시 금일 스팀가격 기준으로 약 7.26$가 찍히네요.

3. DCLICK의 작동 방식

https://steemit.com/dclick/@dclick/dclick-an-incentivized-ad-platform-by-proof-of-click-

디클릭의 간단한 사용설명서를 보시면 스팀 기반 다른 댑들과 마찬가지로 자체 3rd 파티 접속 전용 사이트를 이용해서 글을 쓰게 되어있습니다. 이것은 스팀헌트, 트립스팀 이나 테이스팀과 비슷합니다. 단, 차이가 있다면 사실상 특정한 주제의 글을 쓰기 위해서 쓰는 인터페이스가 아닌 일반 스팀글을 쓰고 밑에 광고를 달기 위해서 쓰고 있는 것입니다.

글을 쓰고 나면 글 하단 부분에 자동으로 광고가 삽입되며, 다른 유저가 글을 읽다가 밑에 있는 광고를 클릭했을 시 SMT 코인으로 보상을 지급하는 방식입니다.

많은 분들이 이미 고민하고 계셨겠지만, 그것을 위해 꼭 3rd파티 인터페이스를 써야 하는것인가 인데, 이것을 위해서 삽입가능한 글이나 이미지도 준비중에 있다고 합니다.

현재는 광고주 모집 단계에 있으며 실제로 Dclick을 사용시 Dclick 자체 광고가 나오고 있습니다.

4. DCLICK의 큰 그림

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmWVrHydZLm4RWiJwz3h23GEgur9UiWp15Q9sF27NThapp/image.png) [Source : rawpixel](https://unsplash.com/photos/aOYA7D3fse8)

보통 제가 관심 갖는 프로젝트들은 큰 그림이 어떻게 그려지는지를 보고 소개를 많이 합니다. 코인 생태계를 만들어주거나 자체 그림이 큰 관계로 성공할 수 밖에 없는 프로젝트들이 관심의 대상이죠. 이번에도 디클릭에 대한 글이 약간 늦어진 것은 @kws4679님과 직접 이야기를 해보고 궁금한것을 풀고 글을 쓰기 위함이였습니다.

다행이 @kws4679님께서 연락을 주셔서 제 궁금한 사항을 다 해결해주셨습니다. ^^

디클릭의 큰 그림은 2장짜리 간단한 백서에도 나와 있습니다. 하지만 모두 백서를 읽는 것으니 아니니 간단하게 제가 보고 들은 큰 그림을 설명하겠습니다.

  • Dclick은 스팀 전용인가…
    디클릭은 스팀의 SMT를 기반해서 토큰 이코노미를 쓰고자 합니다. 하지만 사실상 최종 목적은 광고주도 광고자도 스팀도 SMT도 사실상 토큰도 모르도록 하는 것입니다. 가장 큰 장점인 광고주에게 초저비용으로 광고가능하게 하기를 부각시키고 토큰 이코노미를 이용해서 현금화 가능한 보상을 주도록 하는 시스템 개발이 핵심입니다.

  • 스팀 이용자의 한계를 어떻게 벗어날 것인가?
    스팀헌트, 트립스팀, 아티스팀, 테이스팀, 스팀몬스터등이 갖고 있는 가장 큰 어려움은 외부 유저의 유입니다. 이부분에 대해서 디클릭의 생각을 물었더니 해답은 스팀 SMT의 토큰 이코노미만 빌려오고 실제로 스팀잇과 같은 SNS 기능 자체는 부수적으로만 두는 것입니다.

셀봇에 대해 맞다 틀리다를 떠나서 여기서는 그냥 기술적으로 적용시켜서 어떤 현상이 발생하는지만 보겠습니다. 이전에는 셀봇을 했을때 글을 올리자마자 셀봇하는 것이 가장 유리했습니다. 30분 이내로 셀봇시 큐레이션 리워드는 글 작성자, 즉 저자에게 돌아가게 되어있었습니다. 저는 그래서 가끔 저자에게 보상을 더 많이 주기 위해서 일부러 30분 이내로 업보팅 하는 경우가 많았습니다.

HF-20이후에는 15분 이내에 셀봇할 경우 큐레이션 리워드는 전체 풀로 돌아가게 됩니다. 즉, 큐레이션 리워드는 저자에게 안돌아갑니다.

Curation Updates

30-minute Curation Window

Steem account holders (including bot accounts) are currently disincentivized by the Steem blockchain from voting on a post within the first 30 minutes. The earlier a vote is made within the initial 30-minute window, the less curation rewards the voting account receives. This was originally introduced to even the playing field between human curators and bots during a time when the majority of the content on Steem was long-form written content.

While successful, much has changed on Steem since that time. Steem now hosts all kinds of content, and powers hundreds of decentralized applications which dramatically expand the types of content that can be consumed on Steem. Because of this, the community and the witnesses have come to a consensus that the 30-minute rule is taking curation rewards away from human voters who are actively consuming content and voting on material they like. For this reason, the Velocity Hardfork will reduce the curation window from 30 minutes to 15 minutes.

GitHub Issue 1878

Self-Voting Rewards

According to the current blockchain rules, if authors vote for themselves immediately, they get their author rewards, 100% of the curation rewards from their vote, and a portion of the curation rewards coming from everyone who votes for the post after them. Any other curator voting at the same time as the author would get 0% of the curation rewards. This gives the author an unfair advantage over other curators, because the author can earn additional curation rewards through self-voting.

In order to eliminate this advantage, the portion of curation rewards that is not given to the curators due to the early voting penalty will be returned to the rewards pool instead of being awarded to the author, thereby increasing the overall percentage of rewards paid to curators. This will better serve the original mission of the curation rewards budget: to ensure that the Steem blockchain distributes rewards to the most valuable content.

All delegators to Steemhunt are getting a weekly Airdrop of Hunt tokens. In the current moment, you will get about 85~89 Hunt tokens for 100SP delegated. This is changing depending on the total amount delegated to Steemhunt.
![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmSFPesnvzNhG4qdWaCjWZAPw3hbKys8aUFezSEMXfqNMj/image.png)

b. Promotion Contributors

When writing articles about Steemhunt and promoting it on Steemit or Medium, Reddit or Facebook like platforms, the Steemhunt team is reviewing these contributions on a weekly base and airdropping larger amounts of Hunt tokens to the contributor. I have seen Hunt token drops up to 1.000 tokens and more per week. You will have to drop your link at Discord(https://discord.gg/xJtqe5t) in the channel #contributions. Contributions outside of Steemit are higher valued but will need enough engagements (claps, likes, etc.) to receive an airdrop.
![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmSBuAcfNwXLAwrSNxA2TFytjkuPpWJunNqKi3XPnviy4d/image.png)

c. Social Shares


Source: Pinterest

Social Shares are also a very lucrative way to earn Hunt tokens. Just use the share button at the bottom of each hunt page to share it easily on Facebook, Twitter or Pinterest. If you want to use other services, press the last icon on the right (the chain button), and it will create a referral link with your data. Copy and paste that link on different kind of platforms like Reddit, etc.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmW9yeFR92R4JqfAywqtvqKCKK5iHzanq5Dj3cBNSh2336/image.png)

I have as an example created different boards on Pinterest with specific themes and planning to increase the number of boards with cool hunts to engage users on Pinterest liking them.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmYvZqcQSpdqX3DPgaGynv18xQtaGCarHxGgzLuyCGSrbS/image.png)

d. Hunt posts curator

All hunters who upvote hunts on the Steemhunt platform get a daily airdrop of Hunt tokens based on the voting weight and value they have curated hunts. If you have a more SP on in your wallet, this is also a very significant way to increase your Hunt tokens very fast.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmVxD6Wnok6A82UTCNwZkJN4hRC4CTbMbgzowDDKFdaXQc/image.png)

e. Daily Activity Participants

Steemhunt is using Discord(https://discord.gg/xJtqe5t) as the primary communication channel for hunters. The primary purpose is to engage the community with each other. Especially the channel #random and #questions is crowded. Not many know about it, but Steemhunt is also checking all the activities on Discord and is looking for community members actively answering and helping other peoples inquiries. Usually, questions are answered by community members. If there is a system-specific question, the community manager is looking into that question to solve it. A misunderstood conception is that moderators have to answers questions, but that is also a volunteer activity as a very active community member. A weekly manual Airdrop is going to contributors on Discord.

f. Role contributors

Role contributors are the parts where all Hunt token rewards for community members with roles are combined. It includes Influencers, Moderators and me as the Community Manager.

When you install the extension and input your ID on the right side, you will get an airdrop of 100 Hunt tokens for the first time.
