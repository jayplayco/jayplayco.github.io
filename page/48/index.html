[스팀] 스팀용 Keychain 지갑 소개 - 스팀에도 이제 Metamask가 생겼습니다.

![image-1540249035988.png](https://s3.ap-northeast-2.amazonaws.com/dclick/image/jayplayco/1540249035602.png)

안녕하세요, @jayplayco 입니다.

몇일전에 스팀몬스터를 개발하고 계신 @yabapmatt 가 새로운 Chrome extension을 소개했습니다. 그동안 스팀을 이용하면서 언제나 각 페이지에 키를 넣어야 한다는 불편함(?)이 있습니다. 물론 스팀커넥트가 STINC (스팀잇 본사)에서 만든거라서 안전하다고는 하지만, 혹 스팀커넥트가 해킹이라도 되는 날은 사실 대책은 없겠죠. (그런 날은 절대 오지 않기를 ^^)

yabapmatt는 현재 사실상 스몬 개발때문에 정신이 없는 상황이라, @aggroed (스몬 Co-founder)와 협의해 스팀의 Metamask같은 extension 개발을 펀딩하기로 했습니다. 여러분들이 열심히 사준 스몬 카드가 이런 좋은 프로젝트에 투자되고 있습니다. ^^

https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/steem-keychain/lkcjlnjfpbikmcmbachjpdbijejflpcm

1. 사용방법

* 크롬 브라우저를 사용한다. * 현재는 스팀몬스터, 피크몬스터KR과 피크몬스터.com 에 적용되어 있습니다. (빠르게 다른 댑에도 전파되기를) @steemhunt @trips.teem @dclick @tasteem 도 한번 보시면 좋을듯. * [여기서](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/steem-keychain/lkcjlnjfpbikmcmbachjpdbijejflpcm) 익스텐션을 설치한다. * 설치후 자신만의 패스워드를 넣는다. (익스텐션용 패스워드임) * 추가할 계정(여러 계정 관리 가능)을 입력하고 포스팅키나 액티브키를 넣어준다.

2. 현재 사용 가능한 서비스

* https://steemmonsters.com/ , https://peakmonsters.kr/, https://peakmonsters.com/ * 준비중인 서비스 https://steempeak.com/ * 여러 계정 관리 (AES encrypted) * 간편하게 Steem, SBD 송금 (Active Key 입력시만 가능) * 지갑 내역 보기 * Voting Mana와 Resource Credit 확인 (%) * Keychain 지갑 Lock (브라우저 닫을때, 일정 시간후, Device가 lock될때)

앞으로 추가될 기능

* 파워업/다운 * 임대 관리 * 증인 보팅 관리 * 리워드 claim * Firefox와 다른 브라우저 개발.

[Steemhunt] Direct Signup started

Hi, @jayplayco here.
@steemhunt ‘s announcement hit Steemit yesterday. Steemhunt is now offering a direct signup. We were able to see that Steemhunt is stacking the accounts under steemd. There are now 1,821 pending claimed accounts and increasing.

0. Repository

https://github.com/Steemhunt/web

1. Account creation on Steem

To be able to be active on Steem, Steemit or any Steem based D'apps you need a Steem account. Before [HF-20(Hardfork 20)](https://steemit.com/steem/@steemitblog/steem-velocity-hardfork-hardfork-20) you needed to wait for 2-3weeks before your account got activated, or pay at least 3 Steem to start right away. For a Non-Steemian or a Non-cryptocurrency user, these conditions made it really difficult to start with Steem.

Starting with HF-20 it is now possible to create discounted accounts based on Resource Credits. Long story short, if an account has enough Steempower (delegated or owned) it is possible to create accounts.

2. Direct Signup

Before HF-20 easier and faster methods to get an account was, for example, to create a Github account and claim a Steem account with [Utopian-io](http://utopian.io/). Or buy a Starterpack at [Steemmonsters](https://steemmonsters.com/) with Fiat and get an account.

After HF-20, Steemhunt has now created the first direct signup interface. The difference is that a new user can create an account directly on the Steemhunt interface and does not need to jump over to Steemit. Additionally, users can use their newly created account directly on Steemhunt.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmYe2xNVXarE7SYJ3QQYorySscC1Z5RiWqciVEScSyqxkf/image.png)

3. Softlanding on Steemhunt and Steem

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmX1Aw4TpDWuQKapWEpqJrQbQQz8MrnhAZwHvrgmtQqD2u/image.png)

A new user will not really understand in the first moment that the created account at Steemhunt is a “STEEM” account. A real difficulty for new users at Steem is that a lot of information and services are clustered around. Steemhunt is trying to give the users a soft landing on Steem and explaining the most important parts directly at the signup page.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmbqpuhwUpDWYnFyj4qNhwLaab3CEBRhjQcET2D3NwLXkS/image.png)

As an example, the screenshots on the phones above are showing different services based on Steem.

In my opinion, for an even better soft landing, the first explanation would need a much easier and direct explanation what the new user is actually doing. A first section with “You are creating a Steem account on Steemhunt, this is like creating a Google account to be able to login to a lot of cool services based on Steem like Steemit, Dtube, Tasteem, etc.” could be helpful.

4. Meaning for the Steem community

A great part of Steemhunt is, that the team is operating it as Open source.


Source: Steemhunt Github

Other D’apps based on Steem also need new users outside of the community to grow. Services like Tasteem, Tripsteem could easily adapt the source to match their needs. In the end, it will be a “win-win” situation for D`apps and the Steem community when new users can easily be boarded into the Steem economy.

Github: https://github.com/Steemhunt/web/pull/273

5. Next steps

Creating an account at Steem does have a cost. Even with 1.6Mil Steempower, it is at max about 60 accounts a day that can be created without harming the operation. If the Resource Credits cost will increase, the number of accounts will be even lower.
![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmXGYtH1Vs8qSju7JQSKQKYigDXV1EctKuwNkac4FdD3QX/image.png)

Where money is, there is always the possibility of abusing. Steemhunt has implemented a phone number check as a first barrier. A priority is also to implement a system to detect and stop any attempt to milk this direct signup system.

3. Conclusion

The direct signup is one of the most important parts to start active marketing campaigns outside of Steem. Referral marketing, traditional SEM, Facebook marketing and Influencer marketing are the parts that come directly into my mind. If Dclick(Google Adsense like service based on Steem) grows fast enough outside of Steem, Steemhunt could even do marketing at low cost. We are heading to a very bright Steem future!

[Steemhunt] Steemhunt as witness

Hi, @jayplayco here.
@steemhunt made an announcement last Monday that they will start a witness node. Witnesses are not only important because they are running nodes but also have immense influence within, and if they want, outside of Steem.

0. Repository

https://github.com/Steemhunt/web

1. Where is Steem?

One of the main reasons I like Steem as a blockchain is because of the possibilities it has after the release of SMT. I am personally keeping a close eye on relevant SMT projects and especially the ones who can have a direct impact outside of Steem.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmdpaeBtFDFbQsPhWuxD5zTMM1NuA3ewnYMF8bbjXCs35d/image.png) [Source: Blocktivity.info 2018.10.12](http://www.blocktivity.info/)

So let’s have a look into blocktivity.info. We can see here that @dan was quite successful to co-create three of the most active blockchains ever.

  • BTS(Bitshares) has a great AVI(Activity Valuation Index) of 1,271 but as a DEX (decentralized exchange) not directly interesting for me personally.
  • EOS is of course also attractive as it allows already to create coins with smart contracts. AVI is also 62, so the market cap has still some room.
  • BTC (Bitcoin) is the index, and the CUI(Capacity Utilization Index) is almost at max with 90%
  • ETH (Ethereum) has an AVI of only 5, and the biggest issue here is that the CUI is already at 100%
  • Activity Valuation Index - set Bitcoins market cap number of transactions as an index to “1”. A higher number means a low market cap but more activity than Bitcoin. Source: Blockivity.info
  • Capacity Utilization Index - represents the blockchain usage and remaining available capacity. Source: Blockivity.info
![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmeznYivqM99ZZE55GDkhqkdsqBKC3PenrYWMe3fp7NfKY/image.png) [Source: Blockivity.info](http://www.blocktivity.info/)

So what about Steem? We have a very active blockchain and except BTS the highest AVI of 660. We as Steemians are using Steem and co-creating one of the most active blockchains on this planet. Having a CUI of only 0.07%, there is plenty of room of capacity and the low market cap compared to Bitcoin (AVI) gives Steem also the possibility to grow a lot in value.

With the launch of SMT, Steem will have the ability like Ethereum or EOS to create own Token economies, which is a massive advantage for service providers. Not every single service needs a separate and own blockchain, but a functional and stable one to dock on easily.

2. Witness of Steem

![](https://steemitimages.com/0x0/https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmT85F3oD4xRgREm3uDvo2mpsPuU4sCocP1QnUz1qvKeCb/steemhunt-witness.jpg)

An article from @teamsteem is an excellent overview to get a feeling what a witness of Steem is doing. The excerpt below from “What Are Steem Witnesses And Why You Should Support Them!”@teamsteem gives an insight on what the primary job of a witness is.

What Witnesses Do!

* Maintain their witness node up (Make sure it's running 24/7.) * Maintain their price feed, APR, and registration fee up-to-date (Less important for a backup witness.) * Maintain Steem full nodes * Update their witness node (Only if they agree with the proposed hardfork.) * Review the Steem code and new proposed hardfork changes if able (Github) * Support the creation of new Steem Apps (Code and financial support.) * Debate the proposed hard forks and every Steem rules new or old * Informed and help the community (Posting, commenting, Steem Chat and Steemspeak.com) * Outreach (Marketing, networking, etc.) * Do what every Steemians do (Upvote, post, comment)

An interesting difference between Steem witnesses and as an example EOS’s Block Producers or Tron’s Super Representatives is that there are less company based witnesses. The reason why this may be important is, that of the outreach possibilities they have compared to individuals.

3. Steemhunt as witness

Steemhunt has mentioned two specific activities in their announcement on which they will concentrate, besides their basic responsibilities.

1. Get companies involved with the Steem token economy at a global level
2. Drive the blockchain to be scalable and user-friendly for Steem D’apps.


Source: Steemhunt.com

Steemhunt is preparing a whole economy based on Hunt tokens. “Review Hunt” and “Idea Hunt” are exiting platforms for companies to laser target their customers and reach the needed influencers. “Idea Hunt” is going to be a Kickstarter on steroids, as the first buyers on the platform are the hunters from Steemhunt buying products with Hunt tokens.

Steemhunt has surpassed the MAU(monthly active user) of 70K which is an incredible number for a blockchain project. But to compete with competitors like Kickstarter, the user number needs to be increased massively.

**Number of total and repeat Kickstarter project backers** ![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmT4ha4Er4j2f1YSPeTrdwyXQTGCQQNxEUWReqQ4P37zuc/image.png) [Source: www.statista.com](https://www.statista.com/statistics/288345/number-of-total-and-repeat-kickstarter-project-backers/)

Kickstarter had already in 2014 more than five million backers (not users) and 1.6 million repeat backers. Kickstarter started in 2009, so the number at their start would be much smaller. But to reach a measurable number of backers at “Idea Hunt,” the total MAU would need to increase to a much higher level. Steemhunt will only be able to reach this goal with an easier onboarding of users, as it is common on non-blockchain platforms.

Steemhunt is already working on a scalable new user sign-up and on-boarding interface directly via Steem D’apps. With this new user sign-up, it will be possible to convert incoming traffic to Steem app users. Also, a significant part would be creating a funnel to transfer these users between different Steem D’apps.

[Steemhunt] Own Sign Up system

Hi, @jayplayco here.
Steemhunt is running smoothly, and RC mana is full again. Most of the hunters are now able to hunt and curate on Steemhunt again. As HF-20 is now “successfully” implemented, it is time to plan the next steps.

0. Repository

https://github.com/Steemhunt/web

1. DAU and MAU

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmaMoQXfkcirm3uo2so3WpukTRAeVRMufx9KQ2XnsCNV2J/image.png)
Steemhunt is snowballing, and after introducing the Social share [Airdrop](https://steemit.com/steemhunt/@jayplayco/steemhunt-category-of-airdrops) the DAU (Daily Active User) and MAU(Monthly Active User) numbers are increasing.

At the beginning of September MAU was on a level of about 10K but is now close to 60K.

2. Next needed steps?

One of the main issues Steem based Dapps has, is to get the attention from users outside Steem. If you have managed it well, the Steem community is very open for new Dapps or SMT projects. But your user number stops at a specific moment because of the limitation of the Steem user base. The difficulty starts when you want to outgrow your service from the Steem user base.

3. Limited User base

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmQsL46nxfZoctnYGhy4Go4A6fDvg5YusVuKaXoNQNBitN/image.png) [Source: @arcange](https://steemit.com/statistics/@arcange/steemit-statistics-20181003-en)

If we have a look into the numbers of active authors, it is stagnating, and the active unique users are also decreasing on a level above 100K per month.

With an MAU of about 60K at Steemhunt, it is needed to get users outside of the Steem economy.

4. Current Status

Steemhunt started already with different activities like Social Share airdrop or promotion contribution airdrops and these activities helped to boost the MAU now up to 60K.

But to convert visitors to users, you will need to get them signed to Steem. You can imagine several points that make this problematic.

  • Waiting time to create an account
  • Difficult and long password system for Non-Blockchain users
  • Login into a different site (Steemit)

Landing pages have a bounce rate of 60-90%. A login site on a separate site with a waiting time for two weeks or even paying for it would result probably into a bouncing rate of 99%.

5. HF20 and RC Mana

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmP4WUJhnoMS35q5yHEvTK1aEfWGWsauFJgtr1nq5w96qz/image.png) [Source: Bailey Heedick](https://unsplash.com/photos/yua5-HkEZus)

With the introduction of HF20 and RC Mana, it is now possible to use this Mana to create accounts. This change is very important for Dapps like Steemhunt. It would have cost at least three Steem for each account to produce it on your own. Based on a user base of 200K accounts, you would need at least 600K Steem. This is about 600K USD in the “current” moment. Five months ago you needed more than one Million USD only for account creations.

Now with RC Mana and enough Steem power (delegated or owned), you can use your RC Mana not only for interactions within the Steem Blockchain. The cost of creating an account is high when compared with other activities as writing an article or upvoting. You can create only a limited number of accounts per day because of your limited RC Mana. But even this limited number of accounts will increase the accessibility of users.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmajfx2TZtpBCfRzGRJmh6oyZpQjtM4yXfQ8Fu93B8r4zk/image.png)

Steemhunt will need a separate Sign Up page that is not forwarding to Steemit to keep the users on Steemhunt. The Sign Up process should be as less irritating as possible to avoid that the user cancel the sign up.

After peaking into the Discord channel, it seems that the new signup process has already been planned and the launch is already next week.

Having limited numbers of accounts per day should also not limit the actions of Steemhunt. I am sure that the team will find an exciting way of gamification to use this limit as an accelerator. It could be based on the thinking of, “High demand creates desire.”

As soon as this “Steemhunt Login System” is ready, Steemhunt will have to start a separate marketing campaign or Airdrop targeting new user acquisition. Some ideas could be the already announced Referral Airdrop or real targeted Facebook marketing. The important part is that it would be now possible to organize and start this kind of activities.

3. Conclusion

With a current MAU of about 60K, a bald goal for this year should be at least to reach an MAU of 300K. A milestone of 300K MAU is going to be an essential step also for the upcoming SMT. We could see a different kind of blockchain economies like EOS and Ethereum being very successful because of their abilities to launch significant and relevant projects. @ned, I am very confident that Steemhunt is and will be one of these projects carrying SMT to a success. We will get the investors interested in Steemhunt and at the end in the Steem Blockchain.

“Keep the hunt on”
