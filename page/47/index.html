[Steemhunt] Ideas for Ideahunt

Hi, @jayplayco here.

As we know Steemhunt is planning to launch a Kickstarter like service on the top of the current Steemhunt community. A short overview of their vision can be found here.

A point mentioned from @project7 at @steemfest was regarding Non-Fungible Tokens (NFT) combining it with a crowdfunding platform. I want to dive deeper into this.

0. Repository

https://github.com/Steemhunt/web

1. Ideahunt the Crowdfunding platform

As Steemhunt is already full of hunters looking the whole time for cool and new products, it would be an ideal place for brands and founders to laser-target their audience and launch the product for crowdfunding on Ideahunt. A co-marketing campaign on Reviewhunt could also be helpful to push the marketing further into a success case. Compared to other crowdfunding platforms, it could be possible that the products would be limited to the category Steemhunt is covering, but that is another story. I would personally see a huge potential not only for gadgets but also for digital products like games, e-publishing, e-art, etc. Steem has already a crowdfunding platform named fundition.io, and fundings like @steemmonsters have been successful there. I think the differentiation would be that fundition.io is covering different categories like charity or education, which would not be the focus for Steemhunt.

2. What is a Non-Fungible Token?

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmbYhGqLimoXTtPYK3vtWf62JywPvHWvv9fNwK8BCPme4n/image.png)

The easiest example for Steemians is @steemmonsters. For Non-Steemians Crypto Kitties could be easier to understand the concept of Non-Fungible Tokens, so-called NFT. Fungible Tokens are like Bitcoins or Ether. Every single Bitcoin has the same value as another Bitcoin. For Steemmonsters or Crypto Kitties, the value of each card or Kitty is unique and thus not interchangeable. Wiki Explanation. One of the interesting parts for NFT is that the value of each token can vary over the time from each other.

3. Steemhunts NFT for Ideahunt

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmZGjepu6ngY1M7YgDxMsvKohLKfK3LkCR3j7zLnzmGfQy/image.png) [Disclaimer : this is just a personal imagination for a NFT]

The easiest way for an NFT is to issue a visual look, which differs from other NFTs. For Ideahunt this could be the crowdfunding itself with final data’s of the crowdfunding. All this kind of data must be written on the blockchain to keep the card data unchanged. What would be needed for an NFT combined with Ideahunt would be like the following:

a. DEX

HUNT tokens can be traded to Steem or SBD on a DEX when SMT will be launched. For Ideahunt NFT Steemhunt needs to create a separate DEX (Decentralized Exchange) from the very beginning to make it possible to trade, sell or buy these NFTs. Additionally, a gifting option would be needed to be able to transfer the NFTs from one account to another.

b. DEX Value in USD

After seeing the success from Steemmonsters, one part that had been a very good decision was to sell the packs, starter packs and cards on the Steemmonsters DEX in USD and convert it internally into STEEM or SBD. This is one of the reasons that Steemmonsters cards are still very stable when Steem is on a bottom value. The Ideahunt NFT DEX should be listed in USD to have a stable value on the token. Even if the DEX itself only supports a conversion into HUNT tokens.

c. Ideahunt Value in USD

This could be controversial, but personally, I see it rather as a positive sign for makers and brands to list the products on Ideahunt in USD and convert the matching value in Hunt tokens based on the current rate. To make it easy to buy, the option for FIAT should be definitely considered with payment gateways like Stripe or Paypal.

d. Defining Types of NFTs

(1) Project Type NFT
As shown above, in the image, an NFT could be issued additionally with a perk when buying it. It could be considered to issue also the Token ID on the physical product (if the maker can do it) to bundle the NFT with the real-life product.
(2) NFT as perk itself
![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmVCVFycSW8CNn7p3XKcDPtkrSGnLtZWh13JQUJoEc5nvz/image.png)

Limited versions of NFTs could be sold separately as a perk, or be issued with a low % when granting Project Type NFTs

4. Finding Value in NFT for Ideahunt

NFTs are normally used for crypto collectibles like Steemmonsters. The question is what could I do with an Ideahunt NFT. Normally you can either breed, combine or fight with your NFTs and gain, change or upgrade your NFTs into something else. The rarity and usefulness decide the market value. It is rather difficult to imagine something like that for the NFT from Ideahunt. But on the other hand, it is not impossible.

Steemhunt has been quite good to engage their community with gamification factors. A card game like Top Trumps where a list of numerical data are compared in each round, could be realized with the NFTs from Steemhunt. A crowdfunding has different values which differ from funding to funding and even between perks and perks.

  • % of funding
  • total funding value (in HUNT tokens)
  • Number of backers
  • Number of backed perks
  • Reached 100% in hours or days
  • Perk Value: xxx Hunt Tokens
  • Crowdfunding running time: xx days
  • Unique Visitors during funding: xxx

A dice before each round could decide which parameter should be compared after the cards have been placed from all players and either winner take it all or a number of hunt tokens could be distributed for winning players.

On the other side, the Ideahunt NFT could be just used as collectibles and undermine the ownership of a limited crowdfunded product in the blockchain. In the end, the market would decide what value those NFTs have.

5. Closing

NFT and Ideahunt looks in the first moment not directly like a match. But if you look closer, it is indeed a refreshing idea. The most challenging part would be to create value not only for hunt tokens but also for Ideahunt NFT. This could be realized either with some gamification factors or giving it value as a collectible itself. We are all looking into a very bright future for Steemhunt!

“Keep the hunt on”

  • 증인 서버에서 나오는 모든 수익은 증인 서버 운영비와 스팀피크 개발 비용에 쓸 예정임.

1. What is the Social Airdrop?

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmd53JRukBwt5KbCr6C8FzRF3uJKVswcHie45cWdiMRggq/image.png) Source : https://steemit.com/steemhunt/@steemhunt/share-cool-hunts-on-your-social-channels-and-get-hunt-tokens-everyday-or-sponsor-report-20

Steemhunt started about two months ago an airdrop which is concentrating on social media platforms driving traffic to Steemhunt from outside the Steem economy. The core of this share is to share cool Hunts hunted by hunters and spread them to your followers like Facebook, Twitter, etc.

2. Finding the right spot

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmUPgEhH2USFB7CwzkmZnqWA8Wbj3bUDes38hs6f5nMgh6/image.png)

A difficulty is, if you don`t really have a large follower base established on different platforms. Otherwise just sharing a link into any forum, platform, etc. is actually spamming it and not welcomed at that platform.

If you are good at it, you could also try to create a new facebook group concentrating on gadgets and cool stuff and use the Steemhunt links for that group. This will have definitely a larger effect on a long way but I found an effective way without disturbing the platform itself but using the mechanism of it. It is Pinterest.

3. How I did it

I’ve created a separate account which has the same name as my Steem name and created different boards with themes that could relate to products hunted on Steemhunt. I came up with special watches, drones, E-bikes, Innovative houses, etc. You could expand these boards with more categories from Steemhunt. Each board includes about 20-25 hunts from Steemhunt.

An important part creating this boards was to keep an eye on the thumbnail from the hunt, as Pinterest is a very visual platform.

As you can see this account is quite new and I have actually no followers at all, but I still get from time to time some traffic send to Steemhunt from these few boards. So how does it come that I could create traffic without even having any followers? It is because of the mechanism of Pinterest. If a user of Pinterest finds a so-called “Pin” good, they can pin it also in one of their boards. And if that user has followers, they would see this new pin in their stream. In that way, I can leverage my posting with the follower power of another user.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmaveHQ6XW7u4JEy3UZ9Qj5qAsWGTAtVBvDemC9W6fWVGV/image.png)

If you would be a better curator on Pinterest than me, this effect could very fast double and triple, as you could get also your own followers.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmWFKfZUwTe5znKaJRjHwoMLWEf1oRAsR1FFHYPSqR9WMD/image.png)

I don’t get social share rewards on a daily basis, more once a week or so but due to the fact that the boards will still exist when I don`t touch them, it is a good source of traffic. Of course, the boards would have more success when I will start to increase the numbers of boards, categories, and frequency of update.

Finding interesting board themes can be achieved also with the tools you have at Steemhunt. Click on one of the tags and you will find how many products they are, the ranking and also related tags. Based on that you can recheck on Pinterest if that could be of interest for users there.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmVPvkzQPVY8rwDnEofbecDVjehN4Guh5n1HfdpR7kLpQt/image.png)

4. Social Airdrop is Hunters Level related

A few days ago Steemhunt decided to apply hunter level-based weights on the effective counts from the traffic lead by social shares. The main reason is that there have been abusing (as always) accounts milking the airdrop. This means it is definitely an advantage when you are an active Hunter on Steemhunt.

URL: https://steemhunt.com/

5. Conclusion

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmStL85JELV7RjvzqQKCbmZA2GpsYxkjGR6iZVCCDGR9bh/image.png)

If you are active you can earn Hunt tokens on many different ways and Social Airdrop is one of the methods to create organic traffic not only to Steemhunt but also to the Steem Blockchain. Good luck for your next hunt and happy pinning~!
