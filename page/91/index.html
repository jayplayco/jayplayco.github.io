Hunter’s comment

After writing an article about Crypto Collectibles I started to look for new ones on my own, as Steemhunt should have all available ones listed and also voted (or not), so we can see and find them later.

This one came up after a google search for Crypto Collectibles.

Chibi Fighters is also on the Ethereum blockchain based on ERC721 non-fungible tokens and as the name is mentioning it already, the focus is on fighting each other.

1) You can find Chibi fighters only if you purchased so called founder tokens. If you have not been a early founding member, than you have to buy them on their open market. Current checked price was about 40USD..

2) Chibi fighters can be bought, sold, traded or even be fused. Accessories are also available like swords or daggers, crowns, etc.

3) Fighting
a) Ring of Death : winner takes all style fight. The loser Chibi fighter will die. At least it will be shown at a so called “halls of the fallen”. (For 40 USD bought.. that is harsh…. but high risk… high return)
b) Battle System : quick and easy on a battlefield. Moves are solved by the server. Winner gets Ether and the owner gets their Chibi back. A save game :)

4) Future Roadmap
There are real time battles planned in the future, which should be fun to do.

**Where to buy?**

Chibi is available at opensea.io 's marketplace.

Thoughts
Quick and casual is the main feeling. Design of characters are ok, not the highest standard but the possibility to play quick and easy seems to appeal. The death match is really scary, as it has a bit feeling of gambling 50%/50%.

What are Crypto Collectables?

[Crypto Kitties were just the beginning-outside link](https://cryptobriefing.com/crypto-kitties-were-just-the-beginning/) [A simple explanation of Crypto Collectibles-outside link](https://medium.com/crypto-simplified/a-simple-explanation-of-crypto-collectibles-8674c4527bd1)

Crypto Collectables are digital goods based on Cryptocurrencies, mostly as Token based on Ethereum. You can normally buy them and after that breed, or fight or add accessories, etc, which depends on the concept of each Crypto collectables. In normal cases each collectable has some feature (or tag) to make it either normal or special and the price is varying based on this kind of sets. Additionally all digital goods are unique, so you will find never the same digital goods in the specific crypto collectables eco system.

So long story short, people buy crypto collectables, try to breed or fight with them to get more and looking for ones where the value of the collectable could increase. Or they just buy it because they love it :)

가장 큰 문제가 이때 발생하는 것은 콘텐츠의 질이 아닌 단지 봇의 사용으로 컨텐츠의 순서가 정해진다는 것입니다. 스팀헌트가 지향하는 컨텐츠의 고퀄화와 정면 충돌하는 상황인것입니다.

2. 그럼 ABV를 하잣! 근데 그게 뭐지?

The account-based voting revolution - @liberosist
Account Based Voting is a Terrible Idea for STEEM - @nealmcspadden

@ned (스팀잇 창시자) 가 한국 투어때 ABV(Account based voting, 어카운트 베이스 보팅)를 언급했으며 그에 따른 장/단점이 있습니다만, 간단하게 하기에 설명을 하겠습니다.

A. 현 스팀의 보팅 시스셈.

  • 간단하게는 스팀 파워가 많으면 보팅 파워도 많으며 이로 인해 보팅시 많은 돈을 줄 수 있다.
  • 문제점 : 보팅봇, 큐레이션 그룹, 큐레이션 트레일, 보팅 풀등. (현 시스템에서는 보팅봇에게 대부분의 스팀파워를 랜딩하는 고래분들도 비간접적으로 해당되겠네요..)

B. 어카운트 베이스 보팅 (ABV)

  • 한개의 계정이 한개의 보팅만 할 수 있음. (1계정 1보팅파워)
  • 문제점 : 계정에 웨이팅 (계급이나 단계적 차별성) 이 없을 경우 스팸 계정도 고래 계정과 같은 힘을 가짐. 위 @nealmcspadden 의 글에서도 그 문제점을 짚고 있습니다.

3. 스팀헌트 팀의 접근방법

로직

  • 한개의 업보팅은 100%의 VP(보팅파워)를 가졌을때 10 포인트이다.
  • 포인트는 헌터가 정한 VP에 따라 감소한다. 예) 50%의 VP 세팅시 5포인트의 보팅을 한다.
  • 화이트리스트: 점수는 최소한 헌팅을 한번이라도 한 헌터만 카운트 한다.

가능한 프로세스 (현재는 예시일뿐, 확정은 아님.

  • 강력한 화이트리스트 관리: 최소한 3번이상 헌트한 헌터, 블랙리스트 헌터 제외 등.
  • 헌터 배지 시스템 : 헌팅 히스토리에 따라 레벨 1 에서 5까지 정함.

Lv.1: 1 헌트 (1 풀보팅: 10 포인트)
Lv.2: 10 일 헌팅 + 10회 업보팅 (1 풀보팅 : 20 포인트)
Lv.3: Lv.2 + 3회 이상 탑10 헌트 (1 풀보팅 : 30 포인트)
Lv.4: Lv.2 + 5회 이상 탑 10 헌트 (1 풀보팅 : 50 포인트)
Lv.5: 30일 헌팅 + 30회 업보팅 + 10회 이상 탑10 헌트 (1 풀보팅 : 100 포인트)
레벨은 헌터가 10일 이상 활동을 하지 않을 경우 낮아질 수 있다. (Lv 5 -> Lv 4 -> …)

4. 이것을 적용할 경우 어떤 일이 일어나는가?

@steemhunt 는 정말 활동을 하는 헌터에가 가산점을 주는 것이며 실제로 헌터로 활동을 해야지만 (화이트리스트) 보팅이 카운트 되는 관계로 봇의 활동이 무의미해 집니다.
배지는 그래도 가능할 스팸성 계정들의 활동을 어느정도 억제를 해줄것이고, 레벨업을 통한 헌터들의 선의의 경쟁도 부추겨줄 것으로 기대됩니다. 현재 스팀과 같이 고래가 탄생하지는 못하는 관계로, 레벨5 헌터들의 보팅도 한계가 있어 고래의 어뷰징도 같이 막는 것으로 보입니다.
이를 바탕으로 봤을때는 한 헌터의 보팅파워는 0-100까지 가능하며 (레벨에 따라) 이 모든 것은 고퀄의 컨텐츠를 생산하는 헌터들에게 최고의 보상이 돌아갈 수 있도록 세팅하는데 의미가 있습니다.

5. 로드맵

첫 간단한 세팅은 다음주부터 테스트한다고 @tabris 님께서 언급하셨습니다. 자세한 데드라인은 아직 정해지지 않았으나 @steemhunt 의 팀이 밤낮없이 일하고 있는것은 그간의 활동을 보시면 아실것이며, 스팀헌트 팀도 추가적인 스케일링 전에 이 사안이 우선순위가 높다고 했습니다.
필자는 이것으로 스팀헌트에서 고퀄의 컨텐츠가 승부를 걸 수 있을 것이라고 봅니다.

[Steemhunt] ABV (Account based voting) in development

Hi @jayplaco here! Some development news from @steemhunt

0. Repository

https://github.com/Steemhunt/


Today we are going to talk about the current situation at Steemhunt, where the quality of the content is not driving the community but the use of bots in the background for a successful hunt.

The Steemhunt team is already aware about this issue and for that reason a github issue has been opened and after talking to the main dev. of Steemhunt (@tabris) the implementation is already planned for the next weeks.

So let`s start to see what the issue is and what AVB would give the Steemhunt community.

1. Find the problem of voting / Bug Fixes

Steemhunt, vote bots, incentives and why they need SMT’s @exyle
Is Steemhunt Abusing? @sirin418 (Text in Korean)
Add Flag Feature on steemhunt @tio

Some users already have found that the current setting can be abused with bots to get easily a higher daily ranking. In fact, the users are doing it on daily base now and it comes to the same issue steemit has in background that not the content is speaking but the steempower.

How does it happen that this post is ranking No.1? The answer is easy if you have a look who is actually voting

The highest voter here is called tribesteemup which is a collective voting bot. And with more than 80% of the total vote it is the highest voting power here. Using this strategy you can actually max out your ROI with relatively small or no investment (dependent on what kind of bot you are using.)

2. Yeah, let`s use ABV, but what is that?

The account-based voting revolution - @liberosist
Account Based Voting is a Terrible Idea for STEEM - @nealmcspadden

@ned has mentioned ABV (Account based voting) on his Korea tour and there are pros and cons on it, but to explain it easily see below,

A. Current voting system.

  • The more steem power you have, more voting power you get, which means you can give more money.
  • problem : bots, curation groups, curation trails, voting pools.

B. Account based voting

  • One account counts has only one voting power
  • problem : without any weighting of an account a spam account has the same power as a whale account. This is what @nealmcspadden is talking about.

3. Approach from Steemhunt team / New Features

github entry : https://github.com/Steemhunt/web/issues/135

Logic

  • One upvote has 10 points with 100% vp.
  • The point proportionally decrease based on the hunter’s set vp. For example, the hunter upvotes with 50% vp, the score will be 5 point.
  • Whitelist: Scores are only be counted by the user who have hunted AT LEAST ONE TIME.

Possible Progress (Just examples and not fixed)

  • More intensive whitelist: counted by the hunters with at least three times, exclude blacklist hunters, etc.
  • Hunter’s badge system: We can level the hunters based on their hunting history, from Lv.1 to Lv.5.

Lv.1: 1 hunts (1 full voting score: 10 poinst)
Lv.2: 10 days hunting + 10 times upvoting the hunting posts (1 full voting score: 20 poinst)
Lv.3: Lv.2 + at least 3 times on top 10 hunts (1 full voting score: 30 poinst)
Lv.4: Lv.2 + at least 5 times on top 10 hunts (1 full voting score: 50 poinst)
Lv.5: 30 days hunting + 30 times upvoting the hunting posts + at least 10 times on top 10 hunts. (1 full voting score: 100 poinst)
The Lv can be downgraded if the hunter does not have any hunting or voting activities more than 10 days. (Lv 5 -> Lv 4 -> …)

4. So what does that mean?

@steemhunt will give only active Steemhunters the possibility to vote (whitelist) and level them with badges to differentiate the reputation within the community. Based on the given voting power% a vote can count from 0-100. (Based on your level) The badge system gives different levels to activate the hunters for better content. All this steps are planned to give active hunters with high quality content the best chance for the highest rewards.

5. Roadmap

It is planned to test the first settings next week, but there is in this moment no official deadline from team @steemhunt , but the main dev. from Steemhunt mentioned that it is very a high priority before scaling to the next stage. So let`s see how this will change the quality of the content at steemhunt.
