[네드] STINC의 미래는?

2틀전 네드가 유튜브에 비디오와 글을 올리면서 거의 800개가 넘는 댓글이 생성되었고 여러가지 의견들이 나왔네요. 주요 핵심은

1. 네드글 핵심 요약

  • 열심히 했는데 스팀 가격이 내려서 어렵다… 으잉?
  • 돈이 너무 많이 들어서 직원의 70%를 자르겠다.
  • 비용 계속 줄이겠다.
  • 앞으로 어떻게될지 아직은 모르겠다.. 으잉?

2. 약 800개의 댓글을 읽고 요약

  • 우선 KR측에서는 @roundbeargames 님이 가장 활발하게 토론에 참여하심. 중요 핵심 내용은 : “네드 이제 그만해라.”
  • KR 중에는 @glory7님과 @ayogom님도 댓글로 참여를 하셨습니다. 기본적으로는 네드 비판에 긍정 메세지입니다.
  • 레퓨 70이상 되시는 분들도 이런 저런 아이디어를 올리셨습니다.
    • 그만둬라
    • 스팀잇잉크를 운영비 싼 곳으로 옮겨라
    • 노드가 비싸면 내가 하나정도는 돌려줄 수 있다. 하드웨는 줘라
  • 일부 의견중, 그래도 이렇게 투명성을 강조하는 것은 좋다.
  • 1년 반 이상은 늦었다.
  • 연간 STEEM API Node를 돌리는 들어가는 비용이 연간 2백만불이 넘는답니다.

3. 네드의 반응과 전략??

  • 네드는 댓글을 언제나와 같이 몇개 달지는 않았습니다.
  • 댄이 스팀잇을 혼자 만들었다는데 발끈하네요. Frontend는 개발하지 않았다고 합니다. ㅎ
  • 스팀잇잉크가 (STINC)가 사라지면 일지적으로 API 풀 NODE가 사라져서 지금과 같은 스팀 사용은 어렵다.
  • 비용은 진짜 2백만불 든다. (이것 관련하여서는 여러 댓글들이 efficency를 재강조 합니다. 너무 비싸다고..)
  • 아주 중요합니다. SMT 런칭 일정 관련하여 비용 절감에 집중을 함으로써 Timeline에 영향이 간다고 합니다. 어떤 영향이 있을지는 모른다고 합니다.@steemhunt @trips.teem @tasteem @dclick @artisteem 호출합니다.
    소스
  • 이렇게 나와서 비디오도 찍고 글도 쓰고 하는 가장 중요한 이유는 투명성을 정착시기키 위함입니다.
  • 보니까 포기는 아닙니다. 헤쳐나갈 수 있을것으로 생각하고 있습니다. 소스
  • 스팀잇에 광고를 도입하는 것도 생각 할 수 있는 옵션이다. (문제는 이런것은 이전에도 계속 언급만 했지.. 실행된 적이 없다는…) 소스

4. 앞으로 스팀은?

여기서 부터는 제 개인적인 사견입니다. 우선적으로 저에게 중요한 부분은 SMT 일정이 타격을 분명히 받을 것으로 여겨집니다. 네드가 물러나고 안물러나고를 떠나서. 현 시점에서 스팀잇 잉크의 70%가 이미 내보내져서 개발자들도 많이 나갔으리라 봅니다. 이미 4개월 앞으로 다가왔던 SMT 시작이였는데, 앞으로 스팀잇 잉크의 행보를 지켜봐야 할것으로 보입니다. SMT 관련 준비하시는 분들 역시 유심히 지켜보셔야 할 것으로 보입니다.

만약에 지속적으로 네드 측에서 오픈 커뮤니케이션을 하고, 어느정도 비판적인 커뮤니티의 목소리를 듣고, 가장 중요한 것은 delivery를 한다면 신뢰를 회복할 수 있을 것이라고 여겨집니다. 이것은 증인에게 책임을 더 주는 방식이 되던, 커뮤니티에서 도움을 받아 노드를 돌리게 하던, 광고를 팔던 (STINC의 SEO파워는 엄청납니다… 아직 팔아먹지 않아서 그렇죠..) STINC 소유 지분이나 스팀을 팔던 (이때도 STINC의 Dele가 회수될 수 있느 점을 현 SMT 프로젝트들은 감안을 하고 계셔야 합니다…) 방법은 비즈니스적으로 여러가지가 있겠지만 특히 스팀에 직접적인 현금 투자를 많이 하신 KR 분들의 경우 현재도 파워업을 통한 스팀잇잉크가 아닌 스팀에 대한 믿음을 보여주고 있어 미래가 어둡다고만 할 수 는 없습니다. 탈중앙화와 네드가 어떻게 조화를 이룰지 궁금해지네요.

[Steemhunt] Patterns of abusing at Steemhunt

Hi, @jayplayco here!

When building up a service and especially if you have something to give as an example like a reward, there are always attempts to abuse the system. I would like to list up a few of them to show with what kind of abusing you will deal when building a service based on Steem and SMT. But any other system that is rewarding the user in any form will see similar patterns of abusement in their system.

0. Repository

https://github.com/Steemhunt/web


Source: Pawel Janiak

1. Steem reward abuser

Steemhunt is both airdropping Hunt tokens and also rewarding Users with upvotes for their comments and hunts under specific terms. At the beginning of Steemhunt, the ranking was directly coupled with the upvote power. This resulted (with a much higher Steem value at that time) in an upvote for the first rank over 100USD and more. This started a rally for the rank with hunts, where the quality doesn’t matter but only the ranking itself.

a. Ranking abusement

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmQXWqwPKbGUsCYjsVagxoYVib6gQQ5xguocavhsX2tTYC/image.png) [Source: Miguel A. Amutio](https://unsplash.com/photos/ngZ4V-myG5s)

If you have a ranking system that gathers the ranking based on the Steem system it will be at the end whales who get the highest ranking and also the high upvote. Whales could use their self-vote or also bots to boost their ranking to a higher place. And in reality, whales arrived at Steemhunt and took the higher ranks for themselves.

b. Vote 4 Vote abusement

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmXVSi76a8gb8NnHgBh39dJkN6c3wTqspB5jXMh2AS1VdQ/image.png) [Source: Brandi Redd](https://unsplash.com/photos/aJTiW00qqtI)

Sometimes you can see in the Steem economy, that there are rooms, chats, groups where people ask for a vote and give based on the vote a vote back. This may have no big impact in the Steemit economy but for a ranking board, this is pure manipulation as both sides are gaining higher places in the ranking.

c. Circle Voting

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmaJ9Nr1JEz1WLYucfNWRMjPG6ULLeyn2cq3PJfK4itVPp/image.png) [Source: Stanley Shashi](https://unsplash.com/photos/Pm_926lM-xI)

A lot of communities within Steemit are supporting each other, following each other and voting for the content from their communities. Often these communities are based on nationalities or languages. As the same problem here for a ranking board like Steemhunt is, that it impacts directly the ranking where the core is that somebody should only vote if they really like the PRODUCT and not the HUNTER.

d. Alt Account abusement

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmacU8qL8FwDxAmbWVL64dEL1e3exLh9T9kyZQ9mTV4ip2/image.png) [Source: rawpixel](https://unsplash.com/photos/11tff4dfKDo)

Due to a limited Steem upvote weight, Steemhunt needed to adjust the strategy what and how many hunts or comments they can upvote on a daily base. In the current moment, the Steemhunt bot upvotes the top 100 ranking hunts, based on the hunter score. Only one approved hunt per hunter is upvoted per day. Up to two comments are upvoted. These comments have also a ranking, like the hunts, based on the upvotes they get. Only the top 100 ranked comments get an upvote. Source: Steemhunt

e. Countermeasure for Steem abusements

The abusements above where the main reason that the development of an own [ABV](https://steemit.com/utopian-io/@jayplayco/steemhunt-abv-2-0) started. The current development is now on a stage where this ABV is combined with a hunter score and where the hunter score is the basis for a hunters upvote weight in both directions. It means that the hunter score is responsible for the Steemhunt upvoting weight, which is now decoupled from the Steem upvoting weight. Also, the reward from Steemhunt is leveled based on the User Score the hunter gains with their activity on Steemhunt. Alt accounts are heavily effected together with Circle voting based on their activity on steemhunt and penalized on the user score. Additionally, the community reports suspicious activities which are then analyzed manually and banned from the service if needed.

2. Hunt token airdrop abuser

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmSuWF5xpwYNMN4hRd6jtVZqFt6P9MKJrDCFK9qYaNtkPa/image.png)

Steemhunt is running several kinds of Airdrops to keep the hunters engaged and have fun during their hunts. But as always when there is a reward, abuse is not far away. The airdrop for Hunt tokens is based on having active and engaged users and reward them for their engagement. Additionally, it is used to get more engaged users on board. But some users used the system weakness to milk the reward pool.

a. Chrome extension abusement

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmZ9Dokcb55LmZQHZLANQfeUMN72KGajiKo8vQXDyWeKB7/image.png)

To reach more people and give them an easier access to the Steemhunt platform, a Chrome extension had been released. With the release, it was possible to gain Hunt tokens only opening the extension once a day. You needed to input your username (without any posting or active key) into the extension and you got a daily airdrop. Users realized that they can now easily use their alt accounts to gain additional hunt tokens. And is was abused from a lot of users. Even users running nodes within the Steem community abused this airdrop to maximize their gains.

b. Countermeasure for Hunt tokens abusements

Steemhunt decided to revoke the Hunt tokens airdrop for permanently blacklisted users. Users could be blacklisted for the following reasons.

  • Operates multiple alternative accounts to take an advantage for HUNT token airdrops
  • Creates spam posts or comments
  • Plagiarises

Based on this decision almost 3000 accounts have been permanently blacklisted and about 1.4 million Hunt tokens have been revoked.

3. Conclusion

Due to these different abusements and partly successful trials of milking the system the team behind Steemhunt needed to react very fast on every single case. In about 6 months they have iterated the ABV system from 1.0 to 2.0 to a hunter level. It is now melted all together into a hunter score which is taking care of most of the issues mentioned in this article.

Greed can destroy a reward system and if the system does not countermeasure abusements fast enough it will be milked until it does not anymore exist. I think that Steemhunt did a fantastic job creating an ABV system from scratch and adapting to the behavior of the community. It is at the end about leading the community to the picture Steemhunt is drawing. And that is a Hunt token economy earning and spending them, while hunting and buying cool and new products.

“Keep the hunt on”

[Steemhunt] Ideas for Ideahunt

Hi, @jayplayco here.

As we know Steemhunt is planning to launch a Kickstarter like service on the top of the current Steemhunt community. A short overview of their vision can be found here.

A point mentioned from @project7 at @steemfest was regarding Non-Fungible Tokens (NFT) combining it with a crowdfunding platform. I want to dive deeper into this.

0. Repository

https://github.com/Steemhunt/web

1. Ideahunt the Crowdfunding platform

As Steemhunt is already full of hunters looking the whole time for cool and new products, it would be an ideal place for brands and founders to laser-target their audience and launch the product for crowdfunding on Ideahunt. A co-marketing campaign on Reviewhunt could also be helpful to push the marketing further into a success case. Compared to other crowdfunding platforms, it could be possible that the products would be limited to the category Steemhunt is covering, but that is another story. I would personally see a huge potential not only for gadgets but also for digital products like games, e-publishing, e-art, etc. Steem has already a crowdfunding platform named fundition.io, and fundings like @steemmonsters have been successful there. I think the differentiation would be that fundition.io is covering different categories like charity or education, which would not be the focus for Steemhunt.

2. What is a Non-Fungible Token?

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmbYhGqLimoXTtPYK3vtWf62JywPvHWvv9fNwK8BCPme4n/image.png)

The easiest example for Steemians is @steemmonsters. For Non-Steemians Crypto Kitties could be easier to understand the concept of Non-Fungible Tokens, so-called NFT. Fungible Tokens are like Bitcoins or Ether. Every single Bitcoin has the same value as another Bitcoin. For Steemmonsters or Crypto Kitties, the value of each card or Kitty is unique and thus not interchangeable. Wiki Explanation. One of the interesting parts for NFT is that the value of each token can vary over the time from each other.

3. Steemhunts NFT for Ideahunt

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmZGjepu6ngY1M7YgDxMsvKohLKfK3LkCR3j7zLnzmGfQy/image.png) [Disclaimer : this is just a personal imagination for a NFT]

The easiest way for an NFT is to issue a visual look, which differs from other NFTs. For Ideahunt this could be the crowdfunding itself with final data’s of the crowdfunding. All this kind of data must be written on the blockchain to keep the card data unchanged. What would be needed for an NFT combined with Ideahunt would be like the following:

a. DEX

HUNT tokens can be traded to Steem or SBD on a DEX when SMT will be launched. For Ideahunt NFT Steemhunt needs to create a separate DEX (Decentralized Exchange) from the very beginning to make it possible to trade, sell or buy these NFTs. Additionally, a gifting option would be needed to be able to transfer the NFTs from one account to another.

b. DEX Value in USD

After seeing the success from Steemmonsters, one part that had been a very good decision was to sell the packs, starter packs and cards on the Steemmonsters DEX in USD and convert it internally into STEEM or SBD. This is one of the reasons that Steemmonsters cards are still very stable when Steem is on a bottom value. The Ideahunt NFT DEX should be listed in USD to have a stable value on the token. Even if the DEX itself only supports a conversion into HUNT tokens.

c. Ideahunt Value in USD

This could be controversial, but personally, I see it rather as a positive sign for makers and brands to list the products on Ideahunt in USD and convert the matching value in Hunt tokens based on the current rate. To make it easy to buy, the option for FIAT should be definitely considered with payment gateways like Stripe or Paypal.

d. Defining Types of NFTs

(1) Project Type NFT
As shown above, in the image, an NFT could be issued additionally with a perk when buying it. It could be considered to issue also the Token ID on the physical product (if the maker can do it) to bundle the NFT with the real-life product.
(2) NFT as perk itself
![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmVCVFycSW8CNn7p3XKcDPtkrSGnLtZWh13JQUJoEc5nvz/image.png)

Limited versions of NFTs could be sold separately as a perk, or be issued with a low % when granting Project Type NFTs

4. Finding Value in NFT for Ideahunt

NFTs are normally used for crypto collectibles like Steemmonsters. The question is what could I do with an Ideahunt NFT. Normally you can either breed, combine or fight with your NFTs and gain, change or upgrade your NFTs into something else. The rarity and usefulness decide the market value. It is rather difficult to imagine something like that for the NFT from Ideahunt. But on the other hand, it is not impossible.

Steemhunt has been quite good to engage their community with gamification factors. A card game like Top Trumps where a list of numerical data are compared in each round, could be realized with the NFTs from Steemhunt. A crowdfunding has different values which differ from funding to funding and even between perks and perks.

  • % of funding
  • total funding value (in HUNT tokens)
  • Number of backers
  • Number of backed perks
  • Reached 100% in hours or days
  • Perk Value: xxx Hunt Tokens
  • Crowdfunding running time: xx days
  • Unique Visitors during funding: xxx

A dice before each round could decide which parameter should be compared after the cards have been placed from all players and either winner take it all or a number of hunt tokens could be distributed for winning players.

On the other side, the Ideahunt NFT could be just used as collectibles and undermine the ownership of a limited crowdfunded product in the blockchain. In the end, the market would decide what value those NFTs have.

5. Closing

NFT and Ideahunt looks in the first moment not directly like a match. But if you look closer, it is indeed a refreshing idea. The most challenging part would be to create value not only for hunt tokens but also for Ideahunt NFT. This could be realized either with some gamification factors or giving it value as a collectible itself. We are all looking into a very bright future for Steemhunt!

“Keep the hunt on”

1. 왜 스팀픽은 증인을 잘 할 수 있을까?

제가 개인적으로 생각하는 점들을 정리하겠습니다.

  • 스팀픽은 현재 스팀기반의 스팀잇을 가장 잘 표현한 3rd 파티입니다.

  • 증인 서버에서 나오는 모든 수익은 증인 서버 운영비와 스팀피크 개발 비용에 쓸 예정임.

I want to show that the most important part of Steemhunt’s value is that we don’t want to create just a good community. There are already a lot of great communities in Steemit. Lots of platforms just utilized it for advertisement or display ads. This is also good, but it can be much better. So that’s why we invented the Hunt token economy, that promotes both sites, hunters, and makers. Who wants to utilize hunters force as an asset, hunts token will be rewarded to those hunters activities, so hunters keep sharing cool new innovative stuff constantly. That will promote more traffic outside of the Steemit community and will also attract all the makers and companies. If a maker wants to utilize the forces from hunters to boost up marketing, then they’re going to invite more and more users into the Hunt platform. That’s going to reward hunters what will increase more hunters actions. That results in more traffic and more makers. So this is our growth vehicle, and that will make us different from all the others.

That’s why we’re creating the Hunt platform. We’re not going just to staying as a Steemhunt community, but we’re creating Ideahunt and Reviewhunt on top of the Steemhunt community. We are already working on Reviewhunt, and the target launch will be April next year. Hunters always dig out new stuff, and they have a lot of knowledge about tech products. Some makers want to utilize their force to boost up their new product launching. They can suggest a large discount deal for hunters or they can give Hunt (token) rewards to them. Hunters will then check the products, and they’re going to create very high-quality reviews for them.

The biggest benefit for the makers to utilize our platform is that they can involve all the hunters as users. Anyone who was involved in an early startup knows how painful it is to get high-quality users in the very initial stage. This is going to be a very strong platform for those innovative makers who want access to that kind of high-quality content.

Idea hunt is the last milestone in our roadmap. But we strongly believe that this is going to be a game changer as a crowdfunding platform. Ideahunt is a crowdfunding platform on top of the Steemhunt community. We’ve noticed that current crowdfunding platforms have a major issue, especially Kickstarter. An example, if you backed a project and you need to pay right away. Then you wait until the product is shipped or not. You forget about that. We don’t think that this is efficient. If you back a project then you have to increase the value of the product even before they launched. At this moment we can’t disclose all the details but we are going to introduce it very soon. One point is that we are going to utilize Non-fungible token (NFT) transactions for Ideahunt. With NFT an increase of the backer’s actions can increase the value of the new product launch. This is going to be a real game changer in this industry.

Our ambitious plan requires to have lots of strong users. That’s why we initiated a quite unique way of distributing our Hunt token. By distributing 50% of airdrops. The core idea of this plan is that we want to connect token holders to be the users. Lots of ICO’s are based on the fact that token holders are just investors. They’re not likely to be connected to be a user. That’s not what we wanted to do. So that’s why we have a very unique token distribution. Still, around 24% is distributed, so there is plenty of chance to join this airdrop.

We also started a witness node a month ago with an ambitious mission as a witness. We want to make the Steem Blockchain a place that is favorable for Dapps and SMT’s. Then we can invite a lot of companies to be there. So please support us here. When we approach the market for HUNT tokens, we also want to approach the SMT wide protocol. Final goal would be that SMT tokens can be listed together. This is our aim.

We have some interesting news today as we are discussing with DAYBIT, which is a recently launched exchange market, but already almost in the top 15 level, globally. We are discussing to list Hunt token probably at the end of this year or beginning of the next year. Still ongoing communication so we can’t confirm yet, but we’re going to give you very exciting news very soon. Thank you so much

[스몬] 스팀몬스터 뉴비 지원 프로젝트

안녕하세요, @jayplayco 입니다.

이미 많은 분들이 스팀내에서 스팀몬스터 관련 소식을 많이 접하셨으리라 믿고 있습니다. 스팀기반 댑들이 많이 나오면 그만큼 스팀 생태계에는 긍정적으로 적용될것이라고 생각됩니다.

지금까지 스팀 댑들중 현금흐름을 가장 빠르고 많이 확보한 프로젝트가 바로 스팀몬스터인데요, 스팀몬스터 역시 모든 스팀 댑들이 갖고 있는 숙제중 하나인 신규 사용자 유입이 가장 큰 관건입니다.

KR내에서는 이미 많은 분들이 시작을 하셔서 랭킹에서도 좋은 성적을 내고 계시고 실제 지난 시즌 리그에서는 KR에서 1등 상금인 100SBD를 타가는 영예가 있었죠. (@smonster 화이팅!)

결국 가장 중요한 것은 더욱더 많은 분들이 스팀몬스터를 적극적으로 하시는데 있기 때문에 이번에는 뉴비 지원 프로젝트를 시작합니다.

1. 스몬 뉴비지원프로젝트

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmdu5Bi5A32n7PtV7GD8Q8y5hNf82kxWT8rADj58PJdSrg/image.png) [Source: Filip Mroz](https://unsplash.com/photos/oko_4WnoM98)

아직 한번도 스팀몬스터를 시작하지 않으신 분들, 관심은 있었는데 아직 엄두를 못 내신 분들을 KR내에서 모십니다.

2. 지원 방법및 혜택 -[이벤트 마감^^]

  • 여기에 가서 스몬 스타터팩을 삽니다. (상단 BUY에 있음)

    (참고 : 상단 링크로 가입할 경우 피크몬KR의 레퍼럴이 걸려서 들어가며, 추후 이것으로 정말 처음 시작하는것인지 확인할 예저입니다.^^ 저 링크를 안타고 구매하시면 레퍼럴이 안보여서 확인에 어려움이 있습니다 ㅎ)
  • 총 비용 10SBD정도가 들어갑니다. 물론 신용카드로 사셔도 무관하고요 ^^. 스팀 아이디가 없을 경우 필요한 경우 새로운 아이디도 생성해줍니다.
  • 이 포스팅 댓글에 “지원+@아이디”를 적어주십니다.

a. 혜택

  • 새롭게 가입하신 분들께는 뉴비 지원 차원에서 게임을 하실 수 있도록 부스터팩 5개를 보내드립니다. (시가 10USD)입니다.
  • 이걸로 사실상 스몬을 무료로 시작할 수 있는 방법을 열어드립니다. ^^
  • 총 10분을 모십니다. (수정해야했습니다. 팩이 모자르네요 ㅠㅠ)
  • 기간은 이 포스팅의 페이아웃이나 10분 다 모이실때까지입니다.

b. 제한 및 유의사항

  • 기본 플레이어분들의 부계정이나 새로 생성한 계정은 제외하겠습니다. (신규 유입자를 위함이니 ^^)

  • 스팀 외부에서 유입되었을 경우 누구의 추천으로 오셨는지 댓글 확인 부탁드립니다.
