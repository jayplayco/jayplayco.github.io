[Steemhunt] Setting up Pinterest for Social Sharing.

Hi, @jayplayco here.
There are different ways to earn Hunt Coins, but one of the most effective ones is spreading the news about Steemhunt outside of Steem. I would like to show an effective method without having already a ton of followers on platforms but still be able to generate traffic and earn Hunt Coins.

0. Repository

https://github.com/Steemhunt/web

1. What is the Social Airdrop?

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmd53JRukBwt5KbCr6C8FzRF3uJKVswcHie45cWdiMRggq/image.png) Source : https://steemit.com/steemhunt/@steemhunt/share-cool-hunts-on-your-social-channels-and-get-hunt-tokens-everyday-or-sponsor-report-20

Steemhunt started about two months ago an airdrop which is concentrating on social media platforms driving traffic to Steemhunt from outside the Steem economy. The core of this share is to share cool Hunts hunted by hunters and spread them to your followers like Facebook, Twitter, etc.

2. Finding the right spot

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmUPgEhH2USFB7CwzkmZnqWA8Wbj3bUDes38hs6f5nMgh6/image.png)

A difficulty is, if you don`t really have a large follower base established on different platforms. Otherwise just sharing a link into any forum, platform, etc. is actually spamming it and not welcomed at that platform.

If you are good at it, you could also try to create a new facebook group concentrating on gadgets and cool stuff and use the Steemhunt links for that group. This will have definitely a larger effect on a long way but I found an effective way without disturbing the platform itself but using the mechanism of it. It is Pinterest.

3. How I did it

I’ve created a separate account which has the same name as my Steem name and created different boards with themes that could relate to products hunted on Steemhunt. I came up with special watches, drones, E-bikes, Innovative houses, etc. You could expand these boards with more categories from Steemhunt. Each board includes about 20-25 hunts from Steemhunt.

An important part creating this boards was to keep an eye on the thumbnail from the hunt, as Pinterest is a very visual platform.

As you can see this account is quite new and I have actually no followers at all, but I still get from time to time some traffic send to Steemhunt from these few boards. So how does it come that I could create traffic without even having any followers? It is because of the mechanism of Pinterest. If a user of Pinterest finds a so-called “Pin” good, they can pin it also in one of their boards. And if that user has followers, they would see this new pin in their stream. In that way, I can leverage my posting with the follower power of another user.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmaveHQ6XW7u4JEy3UZ9Qj5qAsWGTAtVBvDemC9W6fWVGV/image.png)

If you would be a better curator on Pinterest than me, this effect could very fast double and triple, as you could get also your own followers.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmWFKfZUwTe5znKaJRjHwoMLWEf1oRAsR1FFHYPSqR9WMD/image.png)

I don’t get social share rewards on a daily basis, more once a week or so but due to the fact that the boards will still exist when I don`t touch them, it is a good source of traffic. Of course, the boards would have more success when I will start to increase the numbers of boards, categories, and frequency of update.

Finding interesting board themes can be achieved also with the tools you have at Steemhunt. Click on one of the tags and you will find how many products they are, the ranking and also related tags. Based on that you can recheck on Pinterest if that could be of interest for users there.

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmVPvkzQPVY8rwDnEofbecDVjehN4Guh5n1HfdpR7kLpQt/image.png)

4. Social Airdrop is Hunters Level related

A few days ago Steemhunt decided to apply hunter level-based weights on the effective counts from the traffic lead by social shares. The main reason is that there have been abusing (as always) accounts milking the airdrop. This means it is definitely an advantage when you are an active Hunter on Steemhunt.

URL: https://steemhunt.com/

5. Conclusion

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmStL85JELV7RjvzqQKCbmZA2GpsYxkjGR6iZVCCDGR9bh/image.png)

If you are active you can earn Hunt tokens on many different ways and Social Airdrop is one of the methods to create organic traffic not only to Steemhunt but also to the Steem Blockchain. Good luck for your next hunt and happy pinning~!

“Keep the hunt on”

Hunter’s comment

Catan Universe, now featuring the Rise of the Inkas.

Catan started as an traditional board game, developed in Germany and been out for years and had been always a top strategy board game.

There has been a former mobile version of Catan, but this version is a whole new adaptation in 3D look.

If you are not familiar with Catan, long story short. It is a turn by turn strategic board game where you can play with up to 4 players and harvest different kind of resources and build roads and cities. Also trading with other players to reach your goals faster or blocking roads to gain access to better resources is a strategy to win this game.

The most fun is coming when it is played with other human players and with this version there is a online mode to play against players around the world from any platform (PC or mobile).

It is a free to play game and there are in app purchases for mobile versions.

Nostalgia for people who know this game!

[Steemhunt] What is Steemhunt?

Hi, @jayplayco here.
This time I would like to introduce Steemhunt for people coming outside of Steem. It would be great to resteem it not only internally of Steem but also outside on platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and co. :)

0. Repository

https://github.com/Steemhunt/web

1. What is Steemhunt?

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmdmrqQpZKvSTnjwYxz9GcE9MCUpcQckW9hE1ooKEYwKVQ/image.png)

URL: https://steemhunt.com/

Steemhunt is a community-based platform for product influencers. Users here are called hunters. As a hunter, you can hunt cool and new products which compete on a ranking board on a daily basis. Other hunters can upvote the hunts when they like them. Each hunted product has the chance to climb to the top of the daily ranking. The best part is that the upvotes from other hunters are worth something, as you can gain a currency called STEEM(or SBD) with each upvote. If you want, you can change STEEM(or SBD) to Bitcoin (or similar) and then to USD on different exchanges.

If you love gadgets or looking for inspirations, this is the right place to be.

2. I am in, how should I start?

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmQtBgZVBygHjVRxezEvarPzc3rES8MG9AY7o1MgkGXaW2/image.png)

First of all, you will need to signup on Steemhunt.

  • You are creating a so-called STEEM account. Think of it as a Google OAuth like login for multiple services. Compared to Google, you can use on some web pages your Google account to log in. With this STEEM account, you can log in to other STEEM services.
  • Look for the Signup button and click on it.
  • Choose a username
  • Enter your mobile phone number and an SMS will be sent to you
  • Enter the confirmation code received on your mobile phone
  • Confirm it and you will get to a confirmation screen with your password.
  • STOP
    Copy your password, as you will never see it again!!!
  • It is still essential to copy your password and keep it safe.
  • Steemhunt is not able to retrieve your password. If you have copied your password, you can click on continue.
  • You are now able to log in.

3. I have logged in and now?

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmeNmqq5SquciLWVpjJWYcU3Q7YMmPbbSHdF1kQhPrZKnU/image.png)

There are several activities you can do. Go for hunting a new product or look for inspirations and upvote products you like. Or move over to Discord where other hunters are chatting with each other.

a. Hunting a product

Hunting a product is not difficult. Look for cool products and post them according to the posting guideline. A Steemhunt moderator will review your hunt manually, which can take up to 48h and leave a comment if the hunt is verified or not. If not reviewed on the same day it will move to the next day ranking board.

Hunters can upvote your hunt on Steemhunt when not delisted from a moderator. Each upvote does not count with the same weight. There is a hunter level in place which decides how strong a vote is. If you can get votes from seasoned hunters with higher levels, your hunt will have a better chance to rank higher.

Some points you should care about before you start.

  • Don`t look for products that have been hunted. (Use the search function to avoid duplicates)
  • Steemhunt only allows specific categories of products. You can check them here.
  • Don`t copy and paste text from other places.
  • It is recommended to read the Posting Guideline to understand what is allowed and what is not.

b. Upvoting other hunts

If you are not directly looking into hunting products, you can also help other hunters and upvote cool products. Just look for the "Today" section and press the arrow up button for a hunt.
![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmebQZoMupPWt4279yBuSoPJhEKVss5ogLSjMSFNxLSKDb/image.png)

You should not upvote more than ten hunts per day in the first moment. (There is a technical background, but on we will talk about it on a later article)

You can also comment on a hunt you like, but also don`t comment on more than one hunt per day in the beginning. (Yes, also a technical background here)

c. Chatting with other hunters on

Intermediate communication between hunters is happening only partly on the Steemhunt platform. A lot of hunters using a group chat service called Discord. You can join the group chat for Steemhunt [here for free.](https://discord.gg/mWXpgks)

Use the same username you have used for Steemhunt and look for the channels “#questions” if you need to ask something or “#discussions” if you have ideas about Steemhunt or want to read more about what other hunters are thinking about Steemhunt. “#faq” is an excellent channel to give you answers for most frequent questions.

4. I like it, but is there more?

Yes, and congratulation. You are now using a D'app on the Steem Blockchain. And you are mining your first cryptocurrencies with upvoting and hunting products. Understanding the technical background and meaning of the Steem Blockchain is at least a few articles for itself. But for now, have fun using Steemhunt and looking for great products. If you get used to Steemhunt and have more questions, you will find a good list of articles below to start to read.

5. Conclusion

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmQD7QPsFdTy4pwEDnxaBewDXg25ELHG7SjJk3MhRvABKN/image.png)

Steemhunt is a place for Geeks and gadget lovers. And the system is designed to reward good hunters. If you love gadgets, then https://steemhunt.com/ is a site you should bookmark to get inspired daily. And if you are great in spotting new and cool products, you just have found your new hunting ground.

“Keep the hunt on”

[피크몬KR] Loyalty 리워드 시스템과 Credit 소개

안녕하세요 @jayplayco입니다.

피크몬이 그동안 지속적으로 업데이트를 해와서 하기와 같은 기능들이 추가되었습니다.

  • Shift Click 을 통한 벌크 구매 (대량 구매자에게 매우 편합니다.^^)
  • 장바구니 카드 45장까지 추가 가능 (이게 블록체인에 올라가는 max라고 하네요)
  • 배틀 전용 베틀체인
  • 내 배틀체인
  • 내 익스플로러
  • 카드 합치기 기능

가장 중요한 것은 그동안 지속적으로 언급드렸던 Loyalty 리워드 시스템이 피크몬.com에서 시작을 했으며 이제 안정화를 거쳐서 피크몬KR에서도 시작하기에 공지를 드립니다.

피크몬.com 공지

1. 로열티 리워드란?

쉽게 말해서 이익 공유 시스템입니다. 피크몬KR을 통해서 구매를 하실때 마다 피크몬은 수수료를 받아서 시스템 운영비용으로 쓰게 됩니다. 이때 일부 이익을 다시 이용자분들께 돌려드리는 형태입니다. 피크몬이 선택한 보상 방식은 Credit 시스템 입니다. 이 크레디트 시스템에 쌓인 크레디트를 이용해서 추가로 스몬 카드를 구매할 수 있습니다.

2. 어떤 식으로 작동하는가?

피크몬KR에서 팩을 구매하거나 마켓에서 카드를 구매하실 경우 피크몬KR은 그에 따른 수수료를 받습니다. 카드를 판매하실때는 피크몬KR에서 수수료를 받지 않고, 판매되는 장(즉, 피크몬.com, 스몬공홈도 해당)에서 그 수수료를 받게 됩니다. (이것은 판매할때 피크몬KR의 한정된 시장뿐만 아니라 전체 시장에 카드를 쉽게 판매할 수 있도록 연동이 되어서 그렇습니다.)

60일간의 구매 볼륨에 따라 레벨을 나눠서 %가 정해집니다. 그 %에 수수료 이익을 곱해서 크레디트에 쌓아둡니다. 그리고 이 크레디트를 이용해서 추가로 스몬카드를 구매할 수 있습니다.

  • 예시: 지난 60일간 마켓에서 카드 볼륨 600USD 에 팩을 볼륨이 500USD이라고 가정을 하면 총 볼륨이 1100USD 입니다. 레벨은 Elemental Maverick이고 (1000USD Volume 이상) 앞으로 피크몬KR에서 추가 팩이나 카드 구매시 피크몬KR 이익의 10%를 크레디트(Credit)으로 받게 됩니다.

[스팀] 스팀용 Keychain 지갑 소개 - 스팀에도 이제 Metamask가 생겼습니다.

![image-1540249035988.png](https://s3.ap-northeast-2.amazonaws.com/dclick/image/jayplayco/1540249035602.png)

안녕하세요, @jayplayco 입니다.

몇일전에 스팀몬스터를 개발하고 계신 @yabapmatt 가 새로운 Chrome extension을 소개했습니다. 그동안 스팀을 이용하면서 언제나 각 페이지에 키를 넣어야 한다는 불편함(?)이 있습니다. 물론 스팀커넥트가 STINC (스팀잇 본사)에서 만든거라서 안전하다고는 하지만, 혹 스팀커넥트가 해킹이라도 되는 날은 사실 대책은 없겠죠. (그런 날은 절대 오지 않기를 ^^)

yabapmatt는 현재 사실상 스몬 개발때문에 정신이 없는 상황이라, @aggroed (스몬 Co-founder)와 협의해 스팀의 Metamask같은 extension 개발을 펀딩하기로 했습니다. 여러분들이 열심히 사준 스몬 카드가 이런 좋은 프로젝트에 투자되고 있습니다. ^^

https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/steem-keychain/lkcjlnjfpbikmcmbachjpdbijejflpcm

1. 사용방법

* 크롬 브라우저를 사용한다. * 현재는 스팀몬스터, 피크몬스터KR과 피크몬스터.com 에 적용되어 있습니다. (빠르게 다른 댑에도 전파되기를) @steemhunt @trips.teem @dclick @tasteem 도 한번 보시면 좋을듯. * [여기서](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/steem-keychain/lkcjlnjfpbikmcmbachjpdbijejflpcm) 익스텐션을 설치한다. * 설치후 자신만의 패스워드를 넣는다. (익스텐션용 패스워드임) * 추가할 계정(여러 계정 관리 가능)을 입력하고 포스팅키나 액티브키를 넣어준다.

2. 현재 사용 가능한 서비스

* https://steemmonsters.com/ , https://peakmonsters.kr/, https://peakmonsters.com/ * 준비중인 서비스 https://steempeak.com/ * 여러 계정 관리 (AES encrypted) * 간편하게 Steem, SBD 송금 (Active Key 입력시만 가능) * 지갑 내역 보기 * Voting Mana와 Resource Credit 확인 (%) * Keychain 지갑 Lock (브라우저 닫을때, 일정 시간후, Device가 lock될때)

앞으로 추가될 기능

* 파워업/다운 * 임대 관리 * 증인 보팅 관리 * 리워드 claim * Firefox와 다른 브라우저 개발.

3. 사용 비디오

https://youtu.be/YjvpffwHAfg

비디오를 보시면 스몬에 접속하는 화면도 보이고, 스팀을 전송하는 기능도 같이 보입니다.

4. 개발진및 운영진

* Funding : @steemmonsters * 운영진 : @yabapmatt, @aggroed * 개발자 : @stoodkev, @nateaguila(그래픽,UI)

[스팀] Ned가 Destiny프로젝트를 위해 새로 기업을 만듭니다.

얼마전에 HF20 진행시 말썽과 문제가 많아서 네드가 증인분들이나 사용자들과 직접적으로 대화를 비교적 많이 했었습니다. 그런 와중에 이런 저런 정보가 흘러나왔고 이를 분석해서 유추를 했던 분도 계셨습니다.

https://steemit.com/busy/@taskmaster4450/ned-designing-a-new-user-interface-not-steemit

실제로 몇시간 전에 @ned는 공식적으로 현재 스팀 기반 Dapp을 새로 개발하고 있다고 발표했습니다. 현재 프로젝트명은 Destiny. 현재까지 여러가지 정황을 유추해봐서 다음과 같이 정리가 되네요.

  • STINC (현 스팀잇 본사)는 steemit.com을 크게 개발을 하지 않을 생각임. (그런것은 busy나 스팀픽이더 잘함? 으잉?)

  • 새로운 기업을 만들어서 스팀 댑을 만들 예정임=> 이것으 Destiny
  • 새로운 기업의 CEO도 같이 겸임
  • Destiny는 모바일을 우선시하고, 일반 유저가 쉽게 크립토에 온보딩 할 수 있는 시스템일것임. (Cryto 금융 관련도 있을것으로 예상됩니다.)
  • 커뮤니티가 활성화될 수 있는 기능을 가진 Dapp일것임.
  • 사실상 Destiny가 뭘 할지는 미지수로 현재 남겨놓음.
