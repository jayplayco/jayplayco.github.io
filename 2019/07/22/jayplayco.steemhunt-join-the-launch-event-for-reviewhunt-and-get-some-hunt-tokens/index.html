[Steemhunt] Join the Launch Event for Reviewhunt and get some Hunt tokens

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmfY7zbrBcnwoXQriwhtmM8Cfz4RSfxNoxiuisAkuWUQvT/image.png)

Hi, @jayplayco here.

The start for ReviewHunt is coming and @monajam, who is one of the moderators of @steemhunt started a Launch Event with a total reward of 210 Steem and HUNT tokens. Let’s dive into the Event to see how and if you can participate.

  • Original Link: https://steemit.com/steemhunt/@monajam/reviewhunt-launch-event-hunt-token-airdrop-210-steem-reward

0. Background

The article and event post from @monajam gives a great overview about the event and background from ReviewHunt itself, so I would like to recommend to check the post for it. To be able to participate on this event there are a few quests to master, so let’s have a look at it.

1. Contest Quests

You will need to have a Steemhunt wallet, so please make sure that you have logged in to https://steemhunt.com/ with steemconnect and check that your wallet is activated. The HUNT bounty is not send to an ETH wallet but only to the Steemhunt wallet. All quests must be done by orders: Quest 1 -> Quest 2 -> Quest 3 -> Buzz Quest, so without Quest 1, Quest 2 will not be accepted. :) You will need to post a separate posting on Steemit or Youtube and keep the form.

a. Quest 1, Preregister on Reviewhunt and leave a comment on @monajam ‘s article.

  • Register here as hunter or maker HERE
  • Leave the your email address on your post. You can leave it as ja***o@mail.com. You can hide the details, but the team should be able to find your email in their database. HERE THE POST
  • Now that was already 10 HUNT tokens.

b. Quest 2, Join Steemhunts Discord group and say hi! Leave a Screenshot on @monajam ‘s article.

  • Join the Discord Server HERE
  • Move to the channel #coffee-lounge and say “HI~!”
  • Leave a screenshot of your discord screen on your post
  • Now that was again 10 HUNT tokens.

c. Quest 3, Tag your friends on @monajam ‘s article.

  • Tag up to 10 friends on your post and get 2 HUNTS per tagged friend. (Max. 20 Hunts)
  • BTW, the team is going to check if the accounts are real.

d. Buzz Quest

  • Write about Reviewhunt, what it is about, what it is for and what the benefits are.
  • Write it on different channels like Steemit, Medium, Twitch, Instagram, Twitter, Reddit, Youtube, etc.
  • Leave the URL of your Buzz on your post.
  • Now you have earned additionally 50 Hunt tokens.
  • Buzz Quests are going to be addtionally rewarded with Steem for the top 10 winners. (1st place 35 Steem, in total 210 Steem)

2. Example

Start your posting with the following words “This is my entry for the sponsored contest organized by @monajam. You can find the contest here” and paste the requested tasks for each Quest.
