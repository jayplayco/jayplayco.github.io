1. What is an IEO?

IEO means “Initial Exchange Offering”. When an ICO is planned and executed from the project itself, an IEO is done from an exchange, which means that at least one party and layer more was involved to check if a project is legit and workable for an IEO or not. For Steemhunt the first IEO at IDCM was very successful and the second IEO with Probit is also really promising, as it is going to be the first ever IEO where you can buy the Tokens with Steem. I have talked with the Steemhunt team and it seems that they had long talks with Probit and managed to convince them to establish Steem also as trading pair for the IEO at Probit. Thanks to the @probit team!

3. Prepare yourself for the IEO

I got some complaints from potential investors who didn’t know that they need to finish the KYC at the exchange to be able to participate at the IEO. Unfortunately, it takes normally up to 24 hours to finish the whole process for KYC, so it is recommended to start with the signup earlier and finish the KYC process before the IEO starts. The main reason for that is, that the available Qty. at Probit is not that big with 7,5 Million HUNTs and you need to be ready and fast if you want to be one of the first 30 persons who benefit additionally from the special sales event.
