Hi, @jayplayco here.

As announced we have started with our first test tournaments and now have officially uploaded the first of four tournaments for the 10K JJM event.

The following people are responsible for running the event for JJM.

  • @danbain : Game Manager

2. Details

b. About Voters and Analyzers Tournament Details

![image.png](https://files.steempeak.com/file/steempeak/jayplayco/awIjhjLD-image.png)
We need people (strategic evaluators) who can evaluate a strategy ahead of the game. If you are interested in this part, please leave a comment. They're going to play two big roles in the game league (a role for Steemmonsters and a role on Steem)

The first role is to broadcast games at the level of explaining to beginners. Since beginners are the key here, we hope the strategic evaluators are able to stimulate their curiosity and interest. Of course, information on tactical strategies and tips should be central.

The second role is to analyze each player’s performance and strategy when game players are added before the game starts. This includes predictions about the winner. JJM will distribute in total 500 JJM/N evenly to the persons who predict the right winner for each tournament.

3. About JJM

JJM is a token based on steem-engine.com using a side chain of Steem. With a holding Steem Power of 500K SP owned and bought from @virus707, this SP is used in combination with JJM tokens to upvote, reward and distribute dividends out of the JJM project to JJM token holders. Our final goal is to create high-quality content and external revenue from advertisement to bring the Steem blockchain to the next step and help the Dapps within the Steem environment. Please read the article below about JJM token if you need more information.
