The JJM Chart is showing the following data. * Price : 0.1 Steem / JJM * Compared before day: +20,040 JJM (JJM tokens added to the total volume compared to the day before) * Total Qty: 1,439,378 JJM (Total Qty in Market holding without locked-up Qty and found Qty.) * JJM holders: 150 accounts (+1 account) (except the founders' accounts) * The current Market value (except founders Qty.) translates to a holders market cap of 143,937STEEM. * Details can be found under https://passionbull.github.io/jjm-list/ made by @jacobyu
![image.png](https://files.steempeak.com/file/steempeak/jayplayco/ibETZtcA-image.png)

We had yesterday a historic day where the JJM token had been reached the 0.1 STEEM level. The interesting part is that the 2nd of April was a bull day for BTC where the price increase was over 15%, but as you can see on JJM it does not directly react on effects of the crypto market. This is mainly driven because of the low Qty. available of JJM and additionally the low number of accounts holding JJM in the current moment. We will see a larger number of accounts holding JJM as soon as the JJM Steemmonsters tournaments are starting.

![image.png](https://files.steempeak.com/file/steempeak/jayplayco/BhbutBfL-image.png)

The daily overview of the DEX https://steem-engine.com/ is showing JJM as the highest volume in the last 24 hours with 716USD volume. It is compared to other tokens on Steem-Engine much higher, but on the other side compared to larger exchanges in the moment still a “not mentionable volume”. Steemmonsters BETA and ALPHA PACK tokens are moving on a similar level of around 10-20USD per day.
