EARTH - An immersive AR/AI experience & model of our planet

EARTH

An immersive AR/AI experience & model of our planet

Screenshots

![a7dedf5ae4b110397d327e03e56f0bd6_original.gif](https://i.imgur.com/wsLmynM.gif)

Hunter’s comment

Earth globes are something really fascinating.

EARTH has taken this to the next level adding AR and AI per App looking into the globe.

So you can explorer the globe not only in 3D but also 4D within time. That is really cool.

  1. Earth globe scaled at 1:106,300,000
  2. A lot of data points for AI and AR
  3. Carbon Dioxide Distribution across time
  4. Migration of animals in time
  5. Biomes and crops and key plants in the world
  6. changing geology
  7. Climate change
  8. Anthropology and human experience
  9. Weather system.

This project is saying “Shut up and take my money~!”

April - Paint by Numbers iOS App

April

Paint by Numbers iOS App

Screenshots

![april.jpg](https://i.imgur.com/GCQh9Lk.jpg)
![april01.JPG](https://i.imgur.com/HEdnS5U.jpg)
![april02.JPG](https://i.imgur.com/LxIsh33.jpg)

Hunter’s comment

Get stressed? No time at all?
Stop what you are doing and just relax for a second.

There are a lot of coloring books and apps outside, but this one is giving a bit of guidance with numbering on each part. This means that you get better results as when you just start to paint whatever you want.

The basic app is for free and it offers In-App purchases.

You have in the free version hundreds of illustrations which are separated in galleries and also levels. A sharing mode is also available to show what you have done.
